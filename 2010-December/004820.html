I have a GlobalSat BU-353, which has a SiRF chipset. gpsd reports it
as:

"SiRF binary","subtype":"GSW3.1.1LowV_3.1.00.07-C23B1.00 "

I noticed problems after doing a "git pull" and rebuild. New messages
were very slow in arriving at the client, and after from five to 10
messages, would stop entirely. The messages were as much as 40 seconds
apart, but the time stamps were sequential. In other words, the
messages were delayed, not dropped.

Examining the log, I saw several commits related to SiRF on Dec. 17. I
rolled back to the commit just before those:

git checkout -b SiRF-Rollback e69da701b358cc40b6a1b766669c8e204158257b

and rebuilt and re-installed. I do not see the halting delivery noted
above.
