Hey Gary,

The log files are attached. gpsd_log.txt is from gpsd, to generate 
it, I set the log level at ntpshm.c:337 to LOG_SHOUT. In this file, 
the antenna was disconnected at time 1291930710.000 then reconnected 
at time 1291930781.000.

placer_log.txt is raw output from the Placer 450.  In this file, I 
have inserted a text line to indicate when the antenna was 
disconnected and then reconnected.

To clarify this report, It appears that the Placer 450 does not 
report loss of fix correctly. By my understanding of the NMEA 
sentences, it should report loss of fix by setting fix quality in 
GPGGA and/or 3d fix in GPGSA. It does neither, it continues to send 
the same GPGSA and GPGGA sentences (with the same time stamp) while 
the fix is lost.
