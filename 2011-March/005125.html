We entered condition Yellow a week ago. In reviewing the git log, I
find that Thomas Sprinkmeier's March 8 bug fix to GST is the only
core logic change in the last week. On March 5 Jon Schlueter had made
a small change in GSV logging. I'm not worried about either.

On gps_waiting(), POLL_BLOCK, and the possibility of giving gps_read()
a third argument: I've decided to leave things as they are.  Yes, the
third argument to gps_read() could work, but the present arrangement
makes better error checks possible - by putting one between the 
gps_waiting() call and the gps_read() you can potentially catch things 
that went wrong with the internal select(2) in the former.

There are to my knowledge no other open issues that can't wait until
after shipping.  Accordingly, we are now in condition Green for 2.96
release.  This is the last call for any remaining pre-release issues.
