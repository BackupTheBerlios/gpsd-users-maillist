support is now working for you?

Since you work with Ntrip streams, I'm wondering if you can supply us
with something we badly need - better test loads.  Right now I have to
take correct functioning of our RTCM2 and RTCM3 analyzers almost
entirely on faith - I have exactly one (1) verified combination of an

from another analyzer would be hugely useful.
