Yo TGS!

On Sat, 20 Aug 2005, TGS wrote:

> 1 - I am seeing the time being read as 1970.

That means your GPS has not sent a sentence specifying the current
year/month/day yet. GPZDA and GPRMC can provide this.

> 2 - When using the xgps client, it flops between 2D and 3D fixes. is that
> normal? I am in a totally open space, without a tree for miles.

This is normal and known.  Not all sentences from the GPS understand 3D,
like GPRMC.  When gpsd sees one it sets 2D mode.  When a 3D sentence, like
GPGGA is sent, the gpsd goes back in to 3D mode.  This flip flops every
time the two are sent.

There is current discussion on how to work around this.
