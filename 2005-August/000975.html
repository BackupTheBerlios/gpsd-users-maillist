"Belgabor" <belgabor at gmx.de> writes:
> I'm trying to decipher what all this pps fuzz is about. What exactly
> is pps?

It depends on the context.  In GPS work there are two that matter.  It
is either:

    1) Precise Positioning Service
    2) Pulse Per Second

The former is only available to people with special encryption
hardware in their military-issued GPS's (and valid current keys).

In the gpsd realm, it almost certainly is the second.  (Although I
suppose it would be possible, at least in theory, for some bored
military type to attach a military-PPS gps to gpsd.)
