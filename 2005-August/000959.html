It looks like you're compiling with all binary protocols disabled; I'm 
guessing your Makefile is thus not defining BINARY_ENABLE. However, if I 
recall correctly, part of the NMEA support was recently added to an 
#ifdef BINARY_ENABLE section (in gpsd.h, part of the 'driver' union).

My guess is that NMEA_ENABLE is being set properly, but BINARY_ENABLE is 
not, thus causing the necessary 'driver' union to not be included. I 
haven't tested it on my machine, but I'm guessing if I disabled 
everything but NMEA, I'd see the same thing.

Just a guess.
