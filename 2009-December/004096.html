After an IRC conference with Chris Kuethe (aka the project lead's
right arm) and Gary Miller (aka the project lead's left arm), our
plan is to ship 2.90 on December 4th.

We're chasing a few minor issues, but all known blockers are resolved.

The most recent one to fall is the mysterious timelag problem
occasionally reported on SiRF GPSes.  Gary proved by experiment that
you can induce it by running in SiRF binary at 4800bps and make it go
away by switching to 9600.  The details are in a new FAQ entry.

Once again, if your know of an unresolved bug, now would be
a good time to speak up.
