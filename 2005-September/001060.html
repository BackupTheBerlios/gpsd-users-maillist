Hello list members,

I discovered GPSD a few days ago and it looks very interesting. Partly as an
exercise in programming and partly through need, I am trying to construct a
driver for my GPS board.

I used the “blank” driver as a starting point and liberally stole ideas from
some of the other drivers. I have now reached the stage where my driver will
read the output from my board and I can see fix and status messages if I use
xgps or cgps. However, neither of them will show the satellite location
table and I have spent three long frustrating evenings going over various
parts of gpsd itself and xgps to figure out what I may have done wrong.

This evening, I discovered how to talk to gpsd with telnet and got output
streams using the r command. These are NMEA sentences of several types and
seem to have reasonable data in them.

I am keen to find the problem, but I would appreciate a few pointers in the
right direction. I am not sure if the data my driver generates is bad and/or
incomplete or if I am just trying to use xgps wrongly.

I know I have a problem with the quality of the signal to my GPS, because of
my physical location and I will be moving to a new location with a clear sky
in the near future, so maybe the data I get is not up to the job; any ideas
please?
