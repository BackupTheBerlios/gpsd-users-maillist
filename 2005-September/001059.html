Success running gpsd with the Rikaline 6010-x5. All details as asked on the
hardware.html page:

GPS unit: Rikaline GPS-6010-X5

Works: yes (see notes on usbserial below). Fast fix, compared to working
with a rockwell jupiter kit.

Sentences:

GPGGA
GPGSA
GPRMC
GPVTG
GPGSV

chipset gps: SiRF Star II
chipset usb cable: pl2303

url: http://www.rikaline.com/gps_receiver.htm

interface: USB

vendor: Rikaline

tested with: 1.10, 1.90, 2.20

nmea version: 2.2 (according to docs)

notes: the usb cable is a separate item to order. you can also order an
rs232 cable or a pda cable.

Linux and usbserial (pl2303). There was an oops in the 2.4 series for
closing serial devices (which gpsd likes to do). This is fixed in at
least 2.4.31, maybe in some earlier versions (not tested).
