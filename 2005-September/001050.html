Sounds like your BU-303 works fine. can not expect their tech support to
go beyond that.

What version gpsd are you using? 2.30 is current and earlier version have
known bugs.

Try starting up gpsd this way:

	gpsd -nN -f /dev/ttyUSB0 -D 5

Be sure to wait several minutes, the auto-detection can be very time
consuming.  What does the debug output say?  Does it correctlly determine
the baud rate?  Does it see any NMEA?
