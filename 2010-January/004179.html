I found another gpsd client, this one for automotive use.

OBD GPS Logger

It does exactly what it says on the tin. It logs OBDII and GPS data on
Linux, OSX and others.

It can then take that logged data, and write useful output formats[.]

http://icculus.org/obdgpslogger/

I haven't tried it.

OBD is the standard for extracting data from your car. The program logs
both GPS and OBD data together so you can plot the car performance data.
