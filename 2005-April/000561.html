> This leads me to believe I should be using /dev/cu.xxx devices.  Anyone know 
> more about BSD/Mac OS X serial devices, that can make a recommendation?

I'm using the tty devices on openbsd, and most of my other serial programs
do too. Then again, I only have USB serial devices that I add/remove at
will. Then again, I should probably investigate the use of the cu devices
to see if that makes some of my serial hiccups go away. UNIX experience
tells me that you use tty if you're getty waiting for a terminal to call
you, and you use cu... if you're a serial port consumer, trying to connect
to some widget hanging off the serial port.
