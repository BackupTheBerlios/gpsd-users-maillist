When qpeQPS starts gpsd, it too uses the -p flag to pass the port. Have you 
set /dev/ttyS3 (or whatever your system needs) in the qpeGPS preferences? 
Otherwise I seem to remember it defaults to something else (something like 
/dev/gps). The preferences are under one of the tabs; you set it by altering 
the text entry field, and to make it take effect you have to hit return while 
still in the field.
