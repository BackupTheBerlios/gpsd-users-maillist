Ok - time to eat some crow - maybe......

I'm a Gentoo user, and like to use gcc optimization flags......

The debug symbols not working AND the seg faults may be related to the 
optimization flags I'm using on Mac OS X.....

I normally use:
CFLAGS=-mcpu=7450 -O3 -pipe -faltivec -fsigned-char -maltivec 
-mabi=altivec -mpowerpc-gfxopt
CXXFLAGS=-mcpu=7450 -O3 -pipe -faltivec -fsigned-char -maltivec 
-mabi=altivec -mpowerpc-gfxopt

When I added "-g" to this line, at the beginning or the end, I still 
didn't get debug symbols.  In trouble-shooting no debug symbols, I set 
my CCFLAGS & CXXFLAGS to '-g'.

I now have debug symbols, and I haven't seen a seg fault.....
