I have built a small application for my Zaurus with a Holux GM-270 CF
card that sends certain SiRF commands in binary mode.

   http://home.knuut.de/arnim.laeuger/sirf_ctrl-0.1.tar.gz

Among others, it can switch on and off WAAS support in both SiRF binary
and NMEA mode. Please note that especially the NMEA commands might be
specific to the GM-270 device. Nevertheless, the code could be a good
starting point for your investigations.
