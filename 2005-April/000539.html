I want to get 2.21 out the door quickly to get the security-fix stuff deployed.

For those of you who were interested in multi-device support, it's in
SVN head now.  For those of you using only one GPS, you'll notice one
small difference: when you set watcher mode and the daemon acquires a
device, it will ship an F response informing you of the device.

I know of only two urgent bugs:

1) Gary Miller is reporting no data on two GPSes of his.  I suspect
   this may be a version-skew problem.

2) Gene Imes reports that setting static navigation didn't fix his
   crazy-speed problem.

I'm going to work on exposing more control bits via sirfmon in hopes
that Gene can find the right one to solve his problem.  Otherwise, 
we're in feature freeze.
