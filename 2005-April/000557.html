I do not have a Sirf here, but if I did I would try this.

Use minicom (or terminal emulator of your choice) to connect to the
serial port with your GPS.  Turn on "capture mode".  Then all traffic
on the serial port will go to a file.  Manual sent the NMEA to change
modes.  Wait for the trash to end.  Close minicom and send the capture
file to the list.
