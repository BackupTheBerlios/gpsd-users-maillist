Ulrich Voigt <ulrich.voigt at gmx.de>:
> I want to add regression tests for my GPS receivers, but I didn't find any
> description how to create such a test. I looked at
> http://gpsd.berlios.de/hardware.html but there's no comment about this. 
> 
> Can you please give me a hint for this?

Sure.  

1. Capture a log of sentences  from your GPS.

2. Feed it to to gpsfake -1; capture the output, that's
   your checkfile.

3. Now, later on, if you want to check for code regressions, 
   feed the log to gpsfake -1 again,  Compare it with the
   check output.  If they're the same, no regression.
