> Iam new to gpsd an try to get my Rikaline 6010 to work.

How long did you wait?  Try starting gpsd, then run xgps and wait about
15 mins for the GPS to get itself together.  Also, be sure your GPS has
a clear view of the sky.  Even windows block GPS signals unless your
antenna is right against it.

Also, unless power is an issue, use the '-n' option to gpsd.  That will
keep the GPS active all the time, not just when a client asks gpsd for data.
