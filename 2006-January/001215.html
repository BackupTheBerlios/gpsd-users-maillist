Have you tried starting gpsd with a high debug level?  Like:
	gpsd -nN -D 6 /dev/gps

If that does not help then try strace:
	strace gpsd -nN -D 6 /dev/gps

Maybe you have an oddball /etc/nsswitch.conf file?  Mine is zero length
to give me the defaults.
