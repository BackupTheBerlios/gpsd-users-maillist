I wrote a gpsd-tracker that makes a few tests before it "reports"
(logs) anything (time since last report, distance since last report,
difference in the track (predicted location) since last report.).  I
have a version that sends an http request to my server.  I, also, have
a version that logs to PostgreSQL.

Look at the code and let me know what you think?
http://twiki.davisnetworks.com/bin/view/Main/GpsApplications

Here's the good stuff...

my $updatetime=$opt{'s'} || 900; #seconds s=seconds
my $updatedist=$opt{'l'} || 1000; #meters l=location
my $updatetrack=$opt{'t'} || 200; #meters t=track

if (testdefined($fix_last, $fix) || 
    testtime($fix_last, $fix, $updatetime) || 
    testdist($fix_last, $fix, $updatedist) || 
    testtrack($fix_last, $fix, $updatetrack) ) {
  report($trackservice, $fix);
  $fix_last={%$fix};
}
