Yo Tim!

On Tue, 17 Jan 2006, Tim Cwik wrote:

> Is anyone using the gpsd pps driver to feed ntpd?

I also have a GPS18lv running my ntp time server.  Works fine for me pretty
much out of the box.

Here is what I add to my ntp.conf:

# for gpsd
server 127.127.28.0 minpoll 4 maxpoll 4
fudge 127.127.28.0 time1 0.180  refid GPS

# for PPS and gpsd
server 127.127.28.1 minpoll 4 maxpoll 4 prefer
fudge 127.127.28.1 refid GPS1

> Does anyone have any thoughts on how to keep the PPS locked. I assume this is
> necessary to use PPS to discipline the ntpd system.

The NMEA can have several hundred milli-seconds jitter.  Fine for many
purposes but not even as good as NTP over UDP.  In theory the PPS can get
you down to micro-second accuracy.  I have nothing to measure mine
against but it is certainly better then just NTP over UDP for me.
