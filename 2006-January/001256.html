Hi all.

Well, I was having a hard time with the bu303 I just got from the gpsstore.

Under some unspecified circumstances it got 3d fixes, sometimes just
2d, most of the time none at all. Another unit, a garmin etrex got
better fixes all of the time.

Well, I was talking to Gary on #gpsd, and he suggested me to do some
tricks with sirfmon (actually, start it it and say 'n').

Gary: now I am sure, just start 'sirfmon /dev/ttUSB0' then say n. It
quits and the device is in NMEA. The LED is now bright. and it flashes
when it gets a fix.

Now, instead of just garbage, the bu303 spits NMEA. Fine, now I can read them.

I had the same issue as described here:
http://twiki.davisnetworks.com/bin/view/Main/GpsBU303

Now my bu303 is running NMEA, but I though it should work in SiRF.
I see info on xgps when I should.

thanks gary.
