I see reports here of problems compiling 2.29 on slackware 10.1, and I
can report the same thing happens compiling 2.30 on slackware 10.2. 
Here's a patch I found that fixes the problem.  In the file xgpsspeed.c,
just before "#include <Xm/XmStrDefs.h>" insert the line "#include
<Xm/Xm.h>".
