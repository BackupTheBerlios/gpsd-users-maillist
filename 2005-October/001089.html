Somewhat outside of the gpsd target, but for the record, GPS compasses
on ships are quite common these days, just have two antennae at some
distance and measure the dt between the arrival of the same satellite's
signal. repeat for several sats, do some math, and there you are.

e.g.

http://www.raytheon-marine.de/highseas/products/commercial/navigation/satellite_compass.html

http://www.boatus.com/husick/techno09_02.asp

http://www.maritech.com/satellite_compass.htm

http://www.jrc.co.jp/eng/product/marine/product/jlr10/jlr10_outline.html
