> I am trying to cross-compile gpsd for an ARM platform. We use the arm_v5t_le- toolchain.
>
> What are the parameters that I have to give for the autogen.sh and ./configure command to compile it for this platform.
>
> The error message that I am getting when I execute ./configure --target=arm_v5t_le- --host=arm_v5t_le- is as follows
>
You need to use a "host triplet" that is in the form of 'cpu-vendor-os' 
where os can be 'system' or 'kernel-system' 
(http://www.gnu.org/software/autoconf/manual/autoconf.html#Specifying-Target-Triplets). 
What you've used is not in this form and so "configure" can't determine 
for what system it is to compile for, and if it's cross-compiling.

This is interpreted by the scripts "config.guess" and "config.sub"

If you find out what your host triplet should be, but configure can't 
find your compiler, you can in addition provide the name of your 
compiler with CC="/path/to/my/prefix-gcc".

>
