Mark Easton wrote:
> Hi everyone,
> 
> How can I have GPSD operate in both raw "r1" mode to process unknown raw
> sentences and "o" mode to get the handled data? (ie at the same time)

   Open two connections.  Receive GPSD format responses on one, and on
the other just issue "r2" (it that's what you mean) and eat the first
nine (?) characters "GPSD,R=2\n"

   In case you did mean "r1" (NMEA), then whatever starts with "$" is
r1's output, whatever starts with "GPSD" is not.  In this case, you can
use only one connection.
