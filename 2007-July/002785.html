> I have gpsd and xgps installed. Now to figure out which usb ports are which.

dmesg, usbdevs, lsusb, usbctl.... some subset of these should exist on
your OS. They can be used to tell you what device you just plugged in,
what kernel driver claims it.... when you plug in a usb-serial type
device, there's a good chance it'll be visible as something like:
/dev/ttyUSB0
/dev/ttyACM0
/dev/cuaU0
/dev/ttyU0
