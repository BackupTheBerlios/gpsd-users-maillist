A friend of mine has a GPS with integrated compass... only in
extremely rare cases does the gps track actually match the compass
heading. Care must be taken to ensure that the device remains level
and aligned with the direction of travel. It's a neat hack, but we've
not really found a use for it.

I guess I might consider allowing it in pass-through mode, but I'm not
seeing a nice clean way to meaningfully integrate such a data source
in GPSD's normal output.
