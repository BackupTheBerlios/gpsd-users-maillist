On 31/05/11 06:18, vwf wrote:

> Is it possible to configure gpsd for 38400 8N1 (no detection etc.)?

   According to ./configure --help there appears to be a way to disable
autobauding, but there is no mention of that affecting data and stop
bits (I understand your problem is that gpsd latches onto your device at
7N2 when you're expecting 8N1)

  --enable-fixed-port-speed=nnn
                          compile with fixed serial port speed


   If that doesn't help, you will need to submit a bug to the gpsd
bugtracker (still in Berlios?)
