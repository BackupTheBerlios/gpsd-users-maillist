Please try "lock orl $0,0(%esp)" instead of "mfence".  The latter does not 
exist on all i386 hardware, like pentium IIs (quark and nog ?).  Have to 
be conditioned on i386, as %esp is %rsp on x86_64, where I think mfence 
should be unconditionally available.
