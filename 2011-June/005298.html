I'll let you know what I find for actual power usage.

One thing that I have already discovered, the reception of the Venus GPS
blows away any other GPS that I have used.  In my living room all my other
GPS receivers can barely pick up 3 satellites and take considerable time to
get a fix.  The Venus almost always shows 8 - 10 satellites (in xgps) and
has no problem getting a 3D fix in just a few seconds.  Not a very
scientific test, but the difference between the receivers is very obvious.

My other receivers are the Toughbook internal (I believe it is a Marvell of
some kind), a Lowrance LGC-3000, and a Garmin Streetpilot 2620.

I have the Venus paired with an antenna also from Sparkfun (sku: GPS-00464).
