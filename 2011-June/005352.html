Yeah. I love the concept of gpsd for user level plug-and-play GPS 
clients like mapping software.  Plug in "A", plug in "B" and gpsd 
figures out how to connect the two.

But as an embedded developer, each automatic thingamabob represents to 
me nothing more than a chance for something to go wrong.  I'd prefer 
fixed baud rate, and aggressive watchdogging.  I may even forego gpsd at 
some point and build something focused on the needs of untended systems.
