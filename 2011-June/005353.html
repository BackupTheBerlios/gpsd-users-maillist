There have been a couple of iterations in the past of "GPSD is too big
or too complex or too general-purpose for my needs.  I'll build something
lighter." These never end well.

GPSD has an *immense* amount of un-obvious domain knowledge folded
into it.  This is easy to miss because of all the effort we've put
into autoconfiguration, and for other reasons as well.  People who try
to build GPSD replacements invariably think it's a simple job and
invariably end up stalling out, eyebrow deep in an evil muck of
details they didn't realize GPSD was saving them from.

We're actually pretty zealous about supporting embedded here, which is one
reason why we have options to disable the hunt loop. Stick around and
work with us; you'll find us responsive, I think.
