I followed the advice and went partly around gpsd, succesfully:
stty 38400 < /dev/ttyUSB0
mkfifo /tmp/pipe
cat /dev/ttyUSB0 > /tmp/pipe &
gpsd -b -n -N -D3 -F /var/run/gpsd.sock /tmp/pipe >t.dmp 2>&1

This worked fine in a test! Definitely not production, but it showed it
could work (and it showed much/most of my navigation system worked).

I asked Moxa about the internals and hope for a full answer.
