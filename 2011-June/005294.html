Josephine <josephine at huron-data.com>:
> Can gpsd receive the output of another gpsd instance as a feed source?

No.  This has been on my list of features to implement for a while, 
but it won't happen until after we ship 3.0.

> >From my initial attempts it appears that this will not work.  Also the man
> page for gpsd states that data packets from a TCP feed "will be interpreted
> as though they had been issued by a serial device".  This leads me to
> believe that gpsd is expecting raw gps receiver (NMEA 0183) data from the
> feed, not the JSON output of another gpsd.

That is correct.

What needs to happen for this feature to work is for the packet sniffer to
be able to recognize JSON objects as a distinct packet type and simply pass
them up to the client.  Possibly we will want to rewrite the tag field on
the way.
