Yo Brett!

I found the eXplorist USB interface spec here:
	http://www.magellangps.com/en/interfaceSolutions/explorist_IS.asp

Should be most of what you need.

As I recently suspected, the eXplorist by default comes up as a standard
USB storage device (page 3).  So Linux should just work fine with it out
of the box like a thumb drive.  Not sure why your hotplug is not working
for you.  Probably you are not building the required modules.

There is not even an eXplorist storage driver for windows.  There is
a serial NMEA driver for windows, not sure why.

Manual confirms that you need to place the exPlorist in NMEA mode, and
reboot it, before the USB interface for NMEA mode will be seen on your
laptop.  The doc does not specify why they felt the need to ship an
eXplorist specific serial NMEA driver.
