I just saw this message.  I would use a simple perl script like this.

gpspipe -w | perl -e
'while(<>){chomp;if(m/^GPSD,O=([^?].*)$/){$i++;@o=split(/\s+/,$1);$y+=$o[3];$x+=$o[4];print(join("|",$o[3],$o[4],$y/$i,$x/$i,"\n"))}}'
