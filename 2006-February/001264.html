On Fri, 3 Feb 2006, Les Smithson wrote:

> I have a Garmin etrex connected to FC4 on a laptop using a Serial-USB
> Adaptor (Targus PA088). This setup used to work OK with older versions
> of gpsd. Now I've installed gpsd 2.30, the adaptor thinks its a mouse.
> In syslog,  get the error messages:

The wacom driver recently ran in to a similar problem.  The usb-hid
driver thought the tablet was a mouse and grabbed control.  The only way
the wacom folks figured out how to stop it was to mod the wacom driver
to grab it back.

Not sure how they did it, but it involved kernel driver tweaks in the C code.

If you can find out what rs-232<->usb chip is in the etrex you could then
talk to that driver group about grabbing that device in their driver.
