Yo Rajinder!

A good way to debug is just to look at the "first failure" and no futher.

That works real well here.  IF curses.h is really in /usr/include, then
are you sure /usr/include is in your cross compilers default include path?

Try adding it to the command line:
	CFLAGS='-I/usr/include'

That actually does not sseem like the right thing.  If you are cross
compiling then your host system include files may be the wrong ones for
the target system.
