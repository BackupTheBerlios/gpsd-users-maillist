>The only think which makes me wondering: Why does xgps tell the right speed?

The bug in gpsd-2.30 only affects its response to the "V" command 
(which is supposed to report speed in knots, but in fact reports in 
thousands of square metres per nautical mile per second... is this 
the same as furlongs per fortnight? :) ).  W or O output is correct 
(reporting in metres per second).  I expect xgps uses W or O, while 
gpsdrive uses V.
