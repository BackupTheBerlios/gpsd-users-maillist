On Thu, 23 Feb 2006, rexfifo at fastmail.fm wrote:

> What I can't seem to find out, however, is whether the nmea link over usb
> is supported by gpsd.  Anyone?

ALmost all NMEA over USB is by way of an RS-232 to USB converter chip in the
GPS.  Often something like a PL2303.  The only native USB driver in gpsd is
the Garmin USB.  There is nothing like you describe in gpsd.
