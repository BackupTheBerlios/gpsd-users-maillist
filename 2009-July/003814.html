Why would you need to hack it? What are you trying to change? There's
nothing cpu-specific in gpsd; people (including me) have run on ARM
cpus before. Aside from the garmin driver, there isn't much in gpsd
that's tied to a specific OS - as long as there's a reasonably
posix-like environment on the ipod, you should be OK... of course, we
have had problems with select() on OS X before.
