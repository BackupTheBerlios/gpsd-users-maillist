On Wed, 10 May 2006, Sune Pedersen wrote:

> The problem is probably that the PPS pulse is TTL level, but should be
> RS232 compatible instead. Will test with a TTL converter and see if I

Depends on the motherboard, some handle TTL just fine and some do not.
Should be easy to find some terminal software to tell you if the CD is
going up and down.  Be sure your PPS in on the CD pin (DB-9, pin 1).

You can also try debug level five (-D 5) as that should log PPS events
for you.
