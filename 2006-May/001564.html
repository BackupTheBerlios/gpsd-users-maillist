Hi

I've found a GPS chipset not listed on gpsd hardware page.

I've tested with gpsd 2.32 from subversion. It seems to work fine:

Navman Jupiter 20 Chipset (with Firmware from Jupiter 21)
http://www.navman.com/Navman/Templates/productinformation____15665.aspx

It's connected with RS232. By default, NMEA sentences are sent, but
gpsd spot that it supports Sirf-II and change to binary Sirf-II Protocol.
I can change the mode back to NMEA, both protocol work:

n=0 -> NMEA
n=1 -> Sirf-II (default)

Here some example of NMEA output:
