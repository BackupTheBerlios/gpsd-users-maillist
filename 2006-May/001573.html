>   10. Notes on the device

It's not a complete GPS, but a chipset. It's running with an external
gyro on a our self developped board.

The device is configured to send NMEA by default. But gpsd spots that
it support Sirf-II and change to this protocol.
But I can change the mode back to NMEA, both protocols work:

n=0 -> NMEA
n=1 -> Sirf-II (default)
