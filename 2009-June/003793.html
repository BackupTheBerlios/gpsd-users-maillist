Hiya,

Are you SURE that the device uses the PS2 connection to the computer?

My quick read on the internet indicates that the GPS is 'supplied 
with an Universal PS2 cable, and a standard USB connector'.

According to this document 
(http://www.buygpsnow.com/driver/HI-204s/204S.pdf) , the PS2 
connector is some form of RS232 connection and Power supply - so 
you're going to have to look up the document and buy (or make) your 
own adaptor cable. If you are making your own, be aware that the 
output of the GPS may not be proper RS232 levels and that you may 
need to use an interface chip (such as a MAX232) to do levle translation.

I would find it extremely unusual for a GPS to use the PS2 keyboard 
or mouse connection to the PC.

Hope this helps.

ciao, Dave
