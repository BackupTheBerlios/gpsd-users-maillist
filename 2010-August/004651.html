Yo Roderick!

On Fri, 20 Aug 2010, Roderick Medina wrote:

> Thanks. I know this digresses from GPSD discussion but do you know a way
> to force ntpd to trust the GPS and to sync up quickly versus gradually?

Add 'prefer' to the SHM(1) server line in ntpd.conf.

You can try chrony.  Some folks say it syncs faster.  Still can take
hours to get a really good PPS lock.

> The desired application is to take this gps unit into a moving vehicle
> without an internet connection. There won't be any other ntp servers to
> get info from.

Then you really want chrony.  ntpd is not well behaved without continuous
and stable internet access.

Even then, until ntpd or chrony has calibrated your internal clock drift
your number will be a bit off.
