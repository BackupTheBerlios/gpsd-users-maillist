I'm using the openwrt-buildtools from their svn, and I'm building gpsd
out of the package-feeds.
A make first build everything in a build_dir, e.g. in my example
./build_dir/target-mips_r2_uClibc-0.9.30.1/gpsd-2.94/gpsd
and then at a later point prepares the rootfs-image by moving files into
e.g.:
./staging_dir/target-mips_r2_uClibc-0.9.30.1/root-ar71xx/usr/sbin/gpsd

At least thats how i do understand it, i do already apologize if i'm
totally of there.
