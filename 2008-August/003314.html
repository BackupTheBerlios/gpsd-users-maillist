gpsd can read from a fifo.

pc1% gpsfeed+ | nc -l 2947

pc2% mkfifo /tmp/gps
pc2% gpsd -n /tmp/gps
pc2% nc pc1 2947 > /tmp/gps

at this point, you will have an instance of gpsd running on pc2,
reading from the fifo /tmp/gps which is getting data over the net from
pc1...

to simplify matters, why not just run gpsd and gpsfeed on the same box?
