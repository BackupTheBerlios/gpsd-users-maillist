It was some time ago and from my notes I used SVN 4408. There's been a 
few changes since then and I haven't done anything since. But the 
command I used at that time waas:

/usr/local/sbin/gpsd -N -n -D5 /dev/ttyS0

Then from another console, I connected:

$ telnet 127.0.0.1 2947
Trying 127.0.0.1
Connected to 127.0.0.1
Escape character is '^]'.
b
GPSD,B=4800 8 N 1
d
GPSD,D=2007-08-12T22:03:54.00Z
f
GPSD,F=/dev/ttyS0
i
GPSD,I=Generic NMEA


What I found most important with this device was to use "-b" to prevent 
talking to the device. On the Internet I find it is using a Sony Chipset.
