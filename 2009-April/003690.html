The NMEA standard has a list of sentences that it marks as
"deprecated" and not recommended for new devices. I am writing an app
that will connect to all kinds of legacy devices, and would like to
know which devices I need to take trouble to support. Some of them are
obvious: DECCA, TRANSIT, and OMEGA are dead as the dodo (deader,
even), and can be forgotten. LORAN is hanging on by a thread. But what
about the rest? If you have old NMEA or GPS gear you are still using,
please scan the list and tell me if you have seen any of these "in the
wild" lately, and if so, with what device (make & model)

Thanks

Eric
