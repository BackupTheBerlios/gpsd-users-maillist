Hi,

The Garmin 16X seems to output its NMEA message
around 500mS after the PPS positive edge. This is not
specified in the manual but just from observation
and the numbers produced by 'ntpq -p' it looks like
500mS is about right.

With the value of PUT_MAX_OFFSET at 500000 
it is hit or miss whether I get the
"not in locking range". I've increased PUT_MAX_OFFSET
to 800000 for my usage. With this value I have a solid lock.

Is it unreasonable to change PUT_MAX_OFFSET to
a larger value in the source repository? I can understand
not having it greater than 1000000.
