> I checked the documentation for lea-4s and it says module is working on NMEA

You may have noticed that your receive sends $GPTXT messages when you
send it something it can't handle. That's a useful packet signature,
as is "$PUBX,01" and getting "$PUBX,01,..." as a reply.

Have a look at drivers.c - this is where we set up structures defining
different hardware types and their probes.

In the case of the ANTARIS "$PUBX,41,01,03,01,57600,00" would set
port 01 to use
NMEA and UBX in (0x1 + 0x2)
UBX out (0x2)
UART speed is 57600
and disable auto-baud.
