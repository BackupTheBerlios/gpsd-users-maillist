For now the Garmin Binary is USB only.  If it is important to you, and you
can test for me, I could hack it a bit to also handle the serial.  Something
I have planned to do for a while but not seen a good reason for yet.  The
only advantage I have seen so far to the binary protocol is less jitter
in the received time since the messages do not vary in length as much as
the NMEA ones.
