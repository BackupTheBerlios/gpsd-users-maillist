handheld GPS unit unusable. :( It's a Garmin Geko 201[1], tiny thing,

Most of my use for the thing (let's be honest, it's a toy!) is in the 
automobile, and I cannot justify spending more money on it. So now it's 
a toy that's not much fun. :(

I suppose I could tape a plastic bag to the exterior of the car and run

Any suggestions for a workaround which would not entail breaking glass

this case-sensitive address will work too.)



[1] The Geko is a nice unit for a handheld, small but with decent

connection. Check it out at garmin.com if you're interested.
