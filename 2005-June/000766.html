David I S Mandala <davidm at them.com>:
> Sorry for perhaps a stupid question but I'm trying to figure out what
> the hotplug script gpsd.hotplug does.

Arranges for gpsd to be informed whenever an eligible USB device goes
active, so the daemon will autoprobe it to see if there is really a
GPS there -- and if so, add it to its internal, list..
