your problem is probably with kismet, i know that older versions (maybe
current ones too) didn't log positions of detected networks after a
gps-fix had been lost. same goes if it never had a fix.

the unknown sentence isn't a problem here i guess.

A returning ? means you don't have a 3D fix (needs at least 4 fixed
satellites), a simply returns the altitude. i guess this isn't needed
unless you want to do 3D plotting of the networks you've found.

try compiling a newer version of kismet, ubuntu isn't up2date (i'm on
breezy and it isn't)
