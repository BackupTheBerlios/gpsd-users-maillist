That's nothing to worry about. It's just the antaris 4 saying "what? i
don't know what to do with these other sentences..." and gpsd saying
"what? i don't know what to do with that last thing you mumbled."
Purely informational - give it a minute and gpsd and your receiver
will come to some agreement. :)
