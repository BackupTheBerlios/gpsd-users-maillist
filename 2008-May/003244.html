On Sat, 24 May 2008, gpsdosm at trixtar.org wrote:

> Just signed up to this group, I'm having difficulty getting
> a Garmin-USB18 gps receiver going [Toshita-A70 & Suse-10.3].
> It's my first gps ever.

gpsd requires a the GPS kernel driver.

> gpsd: Can't open /proc/bus/usb/devices

gpsd uses /proc/bus/usb/devices to verify if the kernel driver is
installed.  Some distros have stopped providing /proc/bus/usb and
that is causing problems for gpsd detecting if the garmin kernel
driver is installed.

Are you sure the garmin kernel is loaded?  If so maybe we can figure out
another way for gps to verify the kernel driver.  Or maybe you can mount
/proc/bus/usb to work around the problem in the short term.
