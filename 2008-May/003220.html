> I have some problems with NTRIP, particularly with the authentication.

Garmin Binary does not support any DGPS protocol.  You will have to try
this in NMEA mode.  Problem is, Garmin USB devices do not support NMEA,
they only support Garmin Binary.  So they GPS device you have can not
work because it just does not support any DGPS protocol.
