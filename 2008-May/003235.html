> May 21 07:56:55 gpsd[12581]: gpsd: PPS pulse rejected.  cycle: 996097,
> duration: 39987

Those short pulses look like noise on the PPS line to me.  Remember the
GPS-18/LVC does NOT output RS-232 levels ( about -12V and +12V).  It
outputs TTL levels (about 0.8 to 3.0).  Some serial ports can handle it
and some can not.  If the port conforms to the RS-232 spec it will not
be able to handle it, but many vendors adjust to handle TTL.

I would add a TTL to RS-232 level converter just to be sure.
