Hi Chris,

>I would add a TTL to RS-232 level converter just to be sure.

If you use a level converter such as the MAX232 (family), this will also 
invert the 'sense' of the PPS pulse from the 18LVC. You may need to change 
the length of the pulse on the 18LVC so that GPSD only sees a short PPS pulse.

If you put TX & RX through the converter as well, you will also need to 
invert each of these lines as the 18LVC outputs RS232 'sense' signals at 
TTL voltage levels.
