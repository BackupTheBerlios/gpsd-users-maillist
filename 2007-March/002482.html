This obviously does not do what you think it will. Specifically, it
tries to use /dev/ttyUSB0 as the control socket, which is probably not
what you want

gpsd -n -N -D 4 -F /var/run/gpsd.sock /dev/ttyUSB0

might be closer to what you need.
