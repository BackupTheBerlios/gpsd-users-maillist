Hi Mick,

I replied to you directly as well as the list.  Sure email me and lets
see what we can manage.  I have got two of these as I mentioned (the
UT+ 8 channel one is driving ntpd right now in NMEA mode), but I also
have running as well the original earlier model (6 channel GT) already
outputs in Motorola binary mode.
This gives us a perfect 'play' vehicle.

Only difference between the two is the later one (UT+) supports an
input source from a differential gps, has 8 channels and a few extra
commands, but the output formats are idential in binary mode.

I am still trying to find my hard copy of the developers
documentation.  I looked and the pdf chapter 6 has all the commands :)
