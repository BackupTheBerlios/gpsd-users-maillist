First off, what version of gpsd are you running? There have been
significant changes in the NMEA parser in the last few months that may
help/explain the issue. See if you can replicate the problem with
gpsd-2.35dev from SVN. There's a snapshot up at
http://pyxis.cns.ualberta.ca/~ckuethe/gpsd-2.35dev.tar.gz if you don't
want to check out from SVN.

If 2.35dev doesn't work, can you go drive around for 15 minutes or
more and log some data? I ask for longer logs because it can sometimes
help to spot bugs. Basically, wait until TomTom says you have a fix,
then use your preferred nmea logging method to record from the raw
device ("cat /dev/rfcomm1 > nmea.txt"  seems to be a popular way to do
this.) Then mail me the log.
