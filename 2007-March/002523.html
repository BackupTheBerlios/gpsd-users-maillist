On 3/27/07, David Cottle <webmaster at aus-city.com> wrote:
> Right now I have a Motorola GT+ 12 channel GPS, its running in NMEA mode
> feeding out GSV, GCA and RMC
>
> Reading the site, its states that running in binary mode it can supply
> differential data for another oncore GT.

shiny!

> My question is will gpsd accept Motorola binary format?  Also what is
> best for use, I am guessing binary is better than NMEA.

We prefer binary protocols - it's generally possible to put a full PVT
solution in a single message, rather than fragmenting it across a
bunch of lines like NMEA does.

A quick grep over the source suggests that we do not yet have a driver
for the OnCore... but we could like to. Our plan is simple: world
domination. Resistance is futile, all receivers will be assimilated.

> If anyone has got one running I would like to hear.  I also have a older
> 6 channel motorola I can use as well.

We'd probably support it if one of us had actual hardware and a
protocol reference. Ebay time, I suppose...
