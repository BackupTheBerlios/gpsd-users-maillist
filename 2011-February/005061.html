On 24/02/2011 12:52, Roger Oberholtzer wrote:
> I have been considering doing the exact same thing. The main thing that
> has kept me from doing this is that I also want access to NMEA records
> in my applications. How does sharing the GPS across apps work
> (ntpd/chrontp and my app)? I know there are apps to share the GPS. But I
> want NMEA from the GPS.

gpsd interfaces with ntpd/chrony automatically via a Shared 
Memory Segment. This does not affect its usual operation, so 
NMEA still streams to applications that request it.

In addition, you may run multiple applications (who use 
libgps) connecting to the same instance of gpsd. In that 
sense, gpsd may be seen as a NMEA multiplexer, among its 
other features.
