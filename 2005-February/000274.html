gpsd no longer has an FV18 driver.  The -T f option is gone.

This is actually good.  What gpsd now does is rapidly sync up with 
7N2 devices (it tries tham after all speeds at 8N1).  The FV18
initialization string is sent shortly after device-connect time to
*any* NMEA device. Because each vendor has its own extension range
in the namespace, the GPS will ignore FV18 initization strings
if it's not an FV18.

Another step on the road to completely removing -s and -T,,,
