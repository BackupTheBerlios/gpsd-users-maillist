A look at socat (http://www.dest-unreach.org/socat/) might be
interesting as it can set up a direct link between two PTYs.
I just tried the following to connect two instances of minicom together:

$ mkdir dev
$ socat PTY,link=dev/gpsd,raw,nonblock \
   PTY,link=dev/nmea_sim,raw,nonblock

This should allow you to hook gpsd to dev/gpsd and feed the
other end at dev/nmea_sim with an application that emits the simulated
NMEA sentences.
Maybe you have to fiddle with the termios options, the above worked well
for minicom.
