A pseudo-tty is something I'd use.  Programs can treat them just like
they treat a serial device.  They are created with openpty(3).  Have a
look through packages that use pseudo-tty's, such as pppd, ttysnoop,
or pptp.  The script(1) command also uses one, as do most graphical
terminal emulators like xterm(1x).
