> From what I can gather, a number of sensors in a yacht, such as
> wind speed, wind direction etc, have an NMEA 0183 interface. There
> are NMEA sentences for this and it seems reasonable to assume these
> are used byt hese instruments.  By reading the documentation for
> gpsd, it is not quite clear to me whether gpsd will limit available
> information to positional parts of the NMEA specification. Will gpsd
> provide any NMEA type output?

Only for sentences it has been taught how to interpret.  Presently
that includes GPRMC, GPGGA, GPGSA, GPGSV, GPGLL, and PGRME.  It could
be taught about others.
