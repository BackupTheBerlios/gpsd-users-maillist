The homepage says that "Onyx also seems to be SiRF-II-based", which is 
not true. Actually there are two Onyx models: RFG-1000 and RFG-2000.
The first one uses some 8-channel chipset, while the second -
UNAV 12-channel (AFAIK without WAAS/EGNOS support),
made of UNAV uN8021C RF frontend and uN8031B baseband processor
http://www.unav-micro.com/data_sheets.htm and
designed by Fastrax Oy http://www.fastrax.fi.
Fastrax claims the 1/4 s message rate support like the ANTARIS from u-blox,
although it is not clear if the "consumer" firmware is capable of that.
The syntax of the $PSFT,ITALK nmea sentence is also not known.
