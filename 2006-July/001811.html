  There's not enough information here to say definitively, but I
suspect the issue is that you do not have the Motif/Lesstif libraries
and/or headers installed on your machine, and the build is failing to
find them.  Try this:

  ./configure --without-x

  Then try the build again.  XWindows can't work as a replacement for
Motif/Lesstif because they are completely different things serving
completely different purposes.  X (XWindows) is your windowing system
and Motif/Lesstif is a library of "window-management functions" (in
much the same way as the Gnome libraries, the KDE libraries, etc.).
Just to be confusing, Motif is also an old-school window manager,
though in this case, that's not relevant.
