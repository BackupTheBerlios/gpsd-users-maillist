You can use perl to ping gpsd as often as your processor will allow
with code like....

perl -MNet::GPSD -e '$g=new Net::GPSD;while($p=$g->get){print
$p->latlon,"\n";}'

But, the update is still limited by the number of messages recieved
from the GPS reciever (readonly) per minute.
