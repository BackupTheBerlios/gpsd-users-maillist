Looks good. Just a few nits to pick:

(1) The "C" in "C/A" stands for "Coarse" (i.e. rough, crude),  not "Course". 
It's spelled wrong in the "architecture.png" diagram.

(2) The sentence which contains "on the time of arrival of the at least three 
different" has a bogus "the". That is, it should say: "on the time of arrival 
of at least three different".

(3) The sentence "Initialization typically set the" is missing the "s" (i.e. 
should be "sets").

And one suggestion:

(1) It might be useful to parenthetically explain that NMEA = "National Marine 
Electronics Association" on the first use of the acronym.
