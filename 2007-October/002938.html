Hi,

I am using a Com-1289 gps board from Parvus using a fastrax  component as GPS receiver.

This card is configured to send NMEA sentences to a serial port (in fact an address on the PC/104 bus linking the two boards) of a motherboard on which GPSd is running (my OS is a Debian).

I use cgps to test this configuration and observe a strange behavior :
    -Longitude and Latitude are always displayed,
    -speed is displayed only a few milliseconds per second, during the other part of the second, it seems the speed is not available.

I analysed NMEA sentences that i received on the serial port, a RMC sentence containing the speed information is received every second.

If you want me to send an extract of received NMEA sentence to this list, let me know.

I do not understand why speed information is not always displayed like longitude and latitude are.
