On Sun, 30 Sep 2007, Michael St. Laurent wrote:

> > gpsd tries hard to avoid command line options,  it might be better off
> > as a configure option.  First how about you check that you are not
> > missing an inverter in your PPS path.
>
> I don't understand why I might be missing an inverter.

TTL to RS-232 level converters almost always invert as well.  So when
you buy a TTL level GPS the signal levels are often inverted due to the
addition or subtraction of an inverter.

>  Is it supposed
> to be the one to zero state change instead of the zero to one?

Sorta hard to tell with a square wave.  Much easier to find the right
edge of a pulse.  If your time matches well with other ntpd peers then
you likely have the proper edge.
