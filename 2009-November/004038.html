Update:

> I got an globalsat EM-406A some months ago, which explicitly should have
> pps output.
>
> http://www.globalsat.com.tw/eng/product_detail_00000055.htm
>
> However, I never got or saw any signal on the pps pin (checked with
> scope).  Except for that it works (which does not make me happier).
>
> I've found one message stating that the PPS signal from a MR-350 only is 1
> us wide:

I have now looked again, and can see the 1 pps signal from my EM-406A with 
a scope.  It is 1 us wide.  But it only shows up in NMEA mode.  When the 
module is switched to SiRF binary, the pulse is gone.

So far, I have not been able to see any trace of the 1 us wide pulse 
inside gpsd.  I've so far only tested with a USB TTL-RS232 cable, which 
may have its own problems with such a short pulse.
