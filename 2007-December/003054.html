Hi, 

maybe libremap.org can save you a trip to the library, see
  http://appdomains.slashgeo.org/article.pl?sid=06/10/30/179233

also for converting state plane coordinates to WGS84 lat/lon
computationally you might use `cs2cs` from PROJ.4,
http://proj.maptools.org

It's pretty easy once you figure out your local EPSG code. But don't
expect to be able to convert from a pre-space age map datum to WGS84
with a degree of precision better than meters.
