Hi!

AFAIK, gpsdrive is no longer maintained by Fritz Ganter, the current 
maintainer is Jörg Ostertag and a few contributors.

As I'm working on a Win32 port of gpsdrive (one of the reasons I was 
asking for / thinking about a Win32 port of gpsd some weeks/months ago), 
I had some contact with Jörg and he told me that the gpsdrive team is 
not really interested in maintaining a "special gpsd branch" - no need 
to reinvent the wheel :-)

I'm currently sucessfully working with a gpsd generated under cygwin 
(BTW: using a Navilock NL-302U). Once the win32 gpsdrive port is ready I 
may even have a look at building a binary Win32 gpsd installer - if you 
like it or not ;-)))

Regards, ULFL
