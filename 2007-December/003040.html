In my previous posting to this thread I suggested a Google Maps interface to
the invitation for comments of qtgpsc but discovered that a full blown
useable general GPS map application for Linux i386 now exists. Its name is
Mapper. Its a port to i386 of Maemo Mapper. I built and ran it on my laptop
and I'm impressed. For now it connects directly to a GPS device. But support
for gpsd is among the goals of the project according to its wiki. It
supports both OpenStreet maps and Google maps.
