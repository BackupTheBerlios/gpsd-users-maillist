> What is the status of GPSD generating RTCM corrections? Is it likely to
> happen soon?

Very, very unlikely.  As I pointed out, this appears to require accurate
timing at nanosecond resolution.  CPUs would need to get 5x to 10x faster 
than they are on any of our target platforms before we can reasonably
expect that, so we're looking at a minimum of four years or so.
