Hi,

I don't know if this is the right list for this kind of information, but
I anyway wanted to report that gpsd works fine with the Navilock model
NL-302U. Maybe you could add the mail address to the hardware page?

Other information:

- product URL:
http://www.navilock.de/produkte/gruppen/3/Kabel_Empfaenger/61422_NL-302U.html?show=spec
- works excellently
- chipset: SiRFstar III (SiRF binary GSW3.2.4_3.1.00.12-SDK003P1.00a)
- interface: USB (using PL2303 chip)
- gpsd version: 2.34 (from Debian unstable)
- NMEA support: GGA, GSA, GSV, RMC, optionally GLL, VTG (but gpsd
switches to the SiRF binary protocol after a few seconds)
- notes: supports DGPS; worked perfectly out of the box!

gpsprof output can be found at
http://www.drbeat.li/album/-Diverses/GPS/Navilock+NL-302U

Thanks for your effort!

Beat Bolli
