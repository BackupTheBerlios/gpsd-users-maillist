On Fri, 14 Dec 2007, Sent by Larry from the Dell Laptop wrote:

> I can't get Kismet to accept input my Garmin GPS-18 USB puck or even to
> acknowledge that it's connected.

Yep, your log shows why.

> gpsd: Can't open /proc/bus/usb/devices

To speak to the Garin USB your need the Garmin USB driver in the kernel.
The kernel driver will then register in /proc/bus/usb/devices.  Since
there is no driver entry found in that file there was no point trying
to open the device.
