Dan Kane <dan at southernkane.com>:
> Having stuff "just work" is the hallmark of good software, as far as I'm
> concerned. Kudos to the entire gpsd team for making this happen!

Actually, the "just works" property is in large part the result of a
happy accident - that pretty much all GPS protocols are
packet-oriented with checksums, so you can reliably distinguish them
from each other and from noise on the serial line.  The central magic
im modern gpsd is a packet-sniffer that selects driver code based on
the flavor of the incoming data.

We will, however, take credit for ruthlessly exploiting this accident, :-)
