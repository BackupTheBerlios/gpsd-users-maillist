> > >  How can I watch both streams at the same time?

I have found a workaround to my problem, but I still hope for a better
solution.

I now start a gpsd daemon connected to the serial port.
I start another gpsd daemon that connect to the first gpsd.
I start another gpsd daemon that also connects to the first gpsd in raw
format.

My application now connects to both secondary gpsd daemons. The first
one gives me the GPS sentences and de second one gives me the AIS
sentences.

The only works because the raw gpsd wil auto magically start the aivdm
driver. While the cooked one will only do NMEA.
