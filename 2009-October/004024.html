Additionally, although not a solution to your problem, I really like 
Chrony over ntp, and with some patches (that are unfortunately not yet 
in upstream I think?) it talks very nicely to gpsd.

In particular it can handle (very nicely) being partially connected to 
various time servers, internet up/down, synchronising the RTC and also 
locks and syncs extremely fast
