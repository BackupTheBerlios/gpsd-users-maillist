I've modified Andrew Ziem's gpsdlog a bit to only log a point every 10
sec (adjustable at the command line), and only the "O" data in .csv
form.
ie:  date|time|timeErr|lat|lon|alt|HorizErr (m)|VertErr (m)|COG|SOG

date|time is local not GMT. License is GPL. Andrew's original was dual
licensed as BSD/GPL.

It is what it is, and I'm not the world's best C programmer, but it does 
its job well.
