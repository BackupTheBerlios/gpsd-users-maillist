What you want is a GIS package.  Commercially, there is MapInfo and
ESRI.  But, Grass and qgis are your open source choices.  Start with
qgis and let us know where you get.  I'm trained on MapInfo so I find
that easiest for me.

I wrote a script GPSDrive2MIF which converts GPSDrive data to MapInfo
formatted data. see
http://twiki.davisnetworks.com/bin/view/Main/GpsApplications

I used GPSDrive to verify the farming acreage of our family farm.  My
father and I drove around each field and then when I imported the data
into a GIS package I was able to calculate the field, pond and tree
acreages.
