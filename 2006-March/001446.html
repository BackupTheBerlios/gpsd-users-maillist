Hi Ravi,

I posted a patch for this bug a day or two ago - the problem is that 
gpsd handles data in "frames" of several NMEA sentences, but was not 
tracking the beginning of frames properly and therefore discarding 
data which arrived at the beginning of the frame.  Let me know if you 
need it re-sent... hopefully it will appear in the svn head soon (or 
it may already have done - I don't have net access right now to check 
it).

Cheers,
