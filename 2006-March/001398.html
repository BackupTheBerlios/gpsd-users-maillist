Hi,

The Open Source Geospatial Foundation is being established as an
umbrella organization in support of open source geospatial software,
covering software projects ranging from those licensed as BSD/MIT and in
wide use in the proprietary world, to leading GPL projects popular in
scientific and consultancy circles. Established sort-of in the image of
the Apache Foundation. The project is just getting started and is mostly
in the organizational phase -- i.e. currently looking for as much
reasoned input as they can get.

https://www.osgeo.org

I encourage you all to go forth and check it out. No idea if there will
be chemistry between gpsd and OSGeo, but I thought I'd make the
introduction.
