Micheal,

  Terraserver has access to Topos and aerial photos for all the US
through a Web Mapping Services (WMS) interface:

http://terraservice.net/ogcmap.ashx?VERSION=1.1.1&SERVICE=wms&request=GetMap&LAYERS=DRG&FORMAT=jpeg&styles=&SRS=EPSG:4326&BBOX=-120.7,41.6,-120.5,41.8&WIDTH=700&HEIGHT=700

The BBOX is a comma delimited list of the x,y coordinates of the
lower-left and upper right respectively. The height and the width
should be proportional to the corner coordinates otherwise images will
be stretched. Also depending on how big your extent is, you'll get
back a 1:24k, 1:100k or 1:250k topo.

If you want aerial photos, you can substitute "DOQ" for the LAYERS parameter.
