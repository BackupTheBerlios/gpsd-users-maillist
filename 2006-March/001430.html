Just a related thought...

In my own code, I've always put the 'units' name right in the variable name 
(e.g. 'gps_ground_speed_knots'). Doing this has prevented any confusion 
whatsoever, over many years of doing this.

I cringe when I see variables with names like 'speed' -- not only do I have no 
idea of the units, but it also makes a search on the variable name turn up 
all sorts of "false positives". Or when I see "miles" with no indication of 
"statute" or "nautical", especially in GPS apps (where they're often both in 
use, at different times).

And I still don't know whether to laugh (at the lunacy and incompetence) or 
cry (there go a lot of tax dollars up in smoke) when I hear of those 
Mars-orbiter-like errors.  :^O
