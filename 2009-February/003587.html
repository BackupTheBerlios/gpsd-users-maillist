The 2.38 release has been shipped and is available on Berlios.
Thanks to all who contributed this time around.

On the agenda for 2.39:

* Making gpssim.py, the GPS simulator, work,

* Support for, and testing of, RTCM3.

* Investigate flakiness near Garmins -- some users report they're
  working, some that they fail.

* Turn sirfmon into a generic tool gopsmon that knows about all the
  GPS types the driver level supports.

* Figure out why it takes so absurdly long to get packet lock on
  Superstar2 GPSes.

* Continue testing and debugging gpsctl -n, which may still suffer
  from timing-dependent glitches.
