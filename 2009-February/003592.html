I just looked at the GPS-60 owners manual and it is straight
NMEA out, so gpsd should see it just fine:

https://buy.garmin.com/shop/store/manual.jsp?product=010-00322-00&cID=144&pID=6446

You should be able to just cat the NMEA from the serial port.
	cat /dev/cu.PL2303
or mayber
	cat /dev/tty.PL2303

Until you can do that no need to start up gpsd.  When you can do that
then gpsd should just work.  Things  to check include baud rate,
parity, etc.

Fire up gpsd like this:
	gpsd -n /dev/ttyPL2303

See if it is working like this:
	cgps
