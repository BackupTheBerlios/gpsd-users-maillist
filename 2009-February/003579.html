oh, and if you are far offshore (eg subantarctic islands) you should not
use NZ Map Grid at all as it becomes highly distorted (ie completely
haywire) far away from the mainland. Instead use NZ Transverse Mercator
with the NZGD2k datum, or something locally defined.

Similarly if you are working with survey-grade GPS coordinates NZMG is
somewhat meaningless for sub-meter resolution and should be avoided.
