Maybe GPSBabel doesn't like the gpsd protocol stuff?  Maybe try
something like

grep '^\$' 2008-11-23-gpsd.nmea >2008-11-23-gpsd-filtered.nmea

to get just the NMEA sentences, then say

gpsbabel -i nmea -f 2008-11-23-gpsd-filtered.nmea

and see what happens.
