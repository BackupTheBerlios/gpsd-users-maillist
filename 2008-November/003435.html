you shouldn't get output. serial devices are not for shared access.

> strace on the process shows a select that runs in a timeout, one of the
> fds is the gps device.
>
> When I stop gpsd I get output on the device again (cat /dev/gps).
>
> The device is a u-blox UBX-G5xxx chip, with an integrated USB-Serial
> interface, detected as ttyACM0 under Linux.
>
> Do you think that is a hardware problem? Or some bug in gpsd?

could be a whole lot of things. turn up the debug level, send it all
to a file and wait 2 days... i don't think it's a gpsd bug, since i
have both ublox and non-ublox receivers that have been connected to
gpsd instances for more than a month. but without more data, it's hard
to know what to look at next.
