Adding to that list of possibly buggy hardware, I have a garmin that,
after a couple of weeks or months of continuous operation, just goes
silent until power cycled. Everything's fine, it's generating
solutions, outputting pulses on the 1PPS line and then it locks up
hard. So it is within the realm of possibility... but without detailed
logs it's tough to be sure.

Like Jan suggests, it could be a driver problem. One thing to check:
once gpsd stops seeing bytes from the receiver, check to see if your
kernel is getting interrupts from the tty and/or usb layers...
