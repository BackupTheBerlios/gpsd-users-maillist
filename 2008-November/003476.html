>
> The GPRS connection of the phone is not reliable enough to allow

> overkill.
>
> I wish to send small UDP packets at intervals of about 1 min, no

I think it would be easier to build a small app that posts your gps
info to a remote host than the other way.
Each and every protocol AFAIK requires some level of handshake to
establish connection, you won't be able to leave that behind :S
