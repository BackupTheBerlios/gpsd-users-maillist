What you're asking is "Plate Carree" projection:
http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Equirectangular_projection

You can just use the lat/lon value.
Position in "meters" doesn't make sense in this instance, as 1 degree lon in
meters at the equator is not the same as 1 degree lon at the pole.

You will have to apply a matrix (i.e. translation/scaling) to the lat/lon
values to get something displayable onscreen (e.g. in the the range (0,
0)x(1024, 768))
