Do yourself a big favor. Do it properly. Use the PROJ.4 library, it's
not too bad and probably a lot less effort in both the short and long
term versus messing around with any number of DIY Mercator projection code
out there (e.g. the "macgps45" source code).


regardless of how you do it you'll have to pick a map projection method
to convert the WGS84 ellipsoid sphere-like surface of the Earth into a
flattened Cartesian coordinate system.

http://grass.osgeo.org/wiki/Gis_Concepts#Geodesy_and_Cartography
