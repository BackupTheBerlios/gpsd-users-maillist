Friends,

I am new to this mailing list.  GPS receiver with gpsd + NTP 
is very helpful/important for my observation network.
Thank you very much for your effort and contribution.

I have two quesiton on NTP with gpsd.

I am using Garmin 18x LVC and for NTP with gpsd.
I am using Ubuntu 9.04.  

(1) NTP ON/OFF behavior
When the NTP reference is gpsd SHM(0) and SHM(1) only, 
the synchronization is ON/OFF frequently.
(SHM(1) is ppt and SHM(0) is GPS serial data)
It is recorded on syslog like this.

May  5 07:52:05 beacon-SRI05 ntpd[3497]: synchronized to SHM(1), stratum 0
May  5 07:52:23 beacon-SRI05 ntpd[3497]: no servers reachable
May  5 07:53:31 beacon-SRI05 ntpd[3497]: synchronized to SHM(1), stratum 0
May  5 07:53:48 beacon-SRI05 ntpd[3497]: no servers reachable
...

The ON time is short, and is sometimes only 1-2 times in an hour.
Under this situation, the PC clock itself looks stable enough.
When the NTP has another network reference, it is very nicely 
synchronized to SHM(1).  Is this behavior correct?  

(2) NTP start problem
At the same condition (NTP only with gpsd), NTP synchronization 
does not start if the PC clock is several minutes (maybe) or 
further off the correct timing.  To overcome this problem, 
I made another small program to set the PC clock to GPS clock 
readings if they are 10s or more different.   
Is this the normal behavior of NTP?
