Note that when you the distance between 2 points with Pythagoras, you
should use the average latitude in the cosine parameter:
x0=cos(lat0 / 2+lat1 / 2)*lon0/M_PI*20000000
y0=lat0/M_PI*20000000

x1=cos(lat0 / 2 + lat1 / 2)*lon1/M_PI*20000000
y1=lat1/M_PI*20000000
