Nice write-up.  A few comments.

gpsd 2.37 has a number of known ntpd and PPS related bugs.  Running
a current version will yeild much better results.

No need to do all the gymnastics to change the GPS baud rate.  You
can do it this way:
	gpsctl -s 38400
