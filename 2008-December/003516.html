I am trying to get an HP 58534A GPS timing antenna to work with gpsd.  The unit does not by default output GPRMC, so in order for me to attach to the nmea sensor in OpenBSD, I can't use ldattach.  The problem that I am running into is twofold.  First, the timing antenna would output GPRMC if only I could get gpsd to output the following init string to the unit:

$PFEC,GPint,GSA01,DTM00,ZDA01,RMC01,GLL00,VTG00,GSV05

This however doesn't happen and I cannot find a way to force it to occur.  Second, gpsd is not interpreting the ZDA sentence, and therefore won't accept GGA due to a missing year and I receive the usual response:

"can't use GGA time until after ZDA or RMC has supplied a year."

Any help is appreciated.  Thanks.
