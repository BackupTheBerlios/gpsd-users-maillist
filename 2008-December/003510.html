On Sun, Dec 14, 2008 at 12:26 AM, drew einhorn <drew.einhorn at gmail.com> wrote:
> A couple of weeks ago, when I asked about garmin_gps, Gary Miller
> commented that garmin_gps needed to be rewritten with libusb,
> but he had neither the time or motivation.
>
> How big a project would this be for a beginner with gpsd?
> Are there well defined APIs at the gpsd and libusb levels,
> with fairly simply algorithms connecting them?
> Or is it deep do do?

for portability and ease-of-debugging, i would suggest that you don't
try to put this driver directly into gpsd. instead, make it write to a
pty, emulating a garmin serial gps.

libusb is pretty well documented. pty/tty isn't too hard either, have
a look at contrib/binreplay.c to see how to open a pty and use it to
pretend to be a serial device.
