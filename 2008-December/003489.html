uh... wtf?

gpsd doesn't open ports/devices/files unless you tell it to. your gpsd
start-up looks ok, though your don't need the '&'. '-n' keeps gpsd
active on the device, '-N' makes gpsd stay in the foreground...
daemonizing into the background is already the default behavior.

perhaps you could trace the binary (ktrace, strace, truss, ...) to see
what system calls it's making. specifically what it's trying to
open().
