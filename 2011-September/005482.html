> Is there a way to command gpsdecode to suppress warning messages that occur
> such as:
> 
>  
> 
> "gpsdecode: invalid AIS channel '2'. Assuming 'B'."
> 
>  
> 
> I can detect and delete them from gpsdecode output but I don't wish to
> recreate a wheel that already exists.

Redirecting stderr to /dev/null should do it, e.g.

gpsdecode 2>/dev/null
