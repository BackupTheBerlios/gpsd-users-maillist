Have you done any calculations of an RS-232 receiver with a PPS signal?

I have been toying with the idea of replacing our locally grown method of syncing various devices and our GPS receiver with gpsd/chrony. The thing that has held me back is that I am unsure what improvements I may get. In my case I have 10 or 100 Hz receivers in a moving land vehicle. I am hoping to get time accuracies of at least 10 mecs. Meaning that my claim of the time at which something occurred is within 10 msecs of the time the GPS would have marked the the data as having occurred. The time accuracy I am getting with the local solution is questionable.
