All,

I updated my FC6 server to F7.  Apart from the gnome sound bug, its
working fine.

However I have a few bugzilla's open that you all may want to reply on
to get some issues solved quickly.

1 - The F7 kernel is not compiled with the 1PPS option.  FC6 kernels
always were.  If we can get more onto thje bug, Fedora will update
future builds to incorporate 1PPS

https://bugzilla.redhat.com/bugzilla/show_bug.cgi?id=242211


2 - selinux.  Major changes in the layout, but they forgot to add in a
ntpd selinux policy.  Means you got to drop from enforcing to
permissive until they add the ntpd daemon back in :(

https://bugzilla.redhat.com/bugzilla/show_bug.cgi?id=242215


Apart from these F7 has been a good experience.  I have upgraded 4
machines from FC6 to F7 without any major drama.


Thanks all!

David
