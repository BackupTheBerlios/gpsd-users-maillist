Yo Hamish!

On Tue, 5 Jun 2007, Hamish wrote:

> is there a known method to change the baud rate of the Garmin binary
> protocol?

Check out the "GPS 18 TECHNICAL REFERENCE" (
425_TechnicalSpecification.pdf) on page 31 (APPENDIX C: CHANGING THE
BAUD RATE IN GARMIN MODE).  That tells how to do this.  It is a painful
13 step process.  I have never found a reason to bother, 9600 is good
enough for 1 fix a second.

Last I checked the file was no longer available on their web site.  I
can shoot you my copy if you need one.
