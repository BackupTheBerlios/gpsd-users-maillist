On Sun, 2006-06-11 at 13:25 -0500, Greg Sahli wrote:
> Hi,
> I'm a newbie. Mandriva 2006, kernel 2.6.12. 
> How do I get gpsd to use pcmcia as the source of NMEA data?

Plug your pcmcia card in and watch your kernel log (or run 'dmesg'
afterwards). Chances are that the kernel detects the card as a serial
device and tells you how to access it. My pcmcia GPS for example
gets /dev/ttyS16 (IIRC, can't check it right now).

Cheers, Til
