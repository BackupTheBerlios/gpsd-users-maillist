> I also have no idea if my gpsd startup is right!

The -f switch is deprecated and not needed.  Try
gpsd -n -D 5 -P /var/run/gpsd.pid /dev/ttyS4

Then check the stdout to see if it's working correctly. Drop the -D 5
after you get it working and the -n if you're concerned about battery.

You can also try to 
stty -F /dev/ttyS4 4800
cat /dev/ttyS4
If you get any NMEA output your at least working.  If you get binary
then it is set to some other protocol.

Also, I looked at the BC-307 on usglobalsat.com and it says it's a CF-I
card not PCMCIA.
