I have released 2.33 on Berlios. Wojciech Kazubski, please build and
release ia32 RPMS.

We have a couple of outstanding bugs.  Tilman Koshnick reports a
problem with the packet-sniffer occasionally locking on 7N2 rather 
than 8N1; Paul van den Berg has shipped a log generated by a 
Magellan eXplorist that crashes gpsd.  Nevertheless, I felt it was
worthwhile to ship a new version to get the recent bug fixes tested.

The two bugs mentioned above will be my priority targets for the 
next release.  Also, I'd like to work with someone who can reproduce
the gpsfake hang reported on the users' list.  Ville Nuorvala thought
he had done so, but it turned out to be due to different versions of
gpsd conflicting.  If there  is a deeper cause behind the other
reports, I want to instrument gpsfake until it becomes apparent.

Particular kudos to Ville Nuorvala this time around for providing
NTRIP support, a significant extension of gpsd's capabilities..
