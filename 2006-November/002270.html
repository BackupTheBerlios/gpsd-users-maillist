There's no garmin voodoo involved. It's just splitting
gpsd_binary_quality_dump() to have it build a PGRME setence if ERR_SET
is set (by garmin.c) because the current condition HDOP_SET for both
GPGSA and PGRME sentence would never be met for garmin devices
(see also parsing of PGRME in nmea_parse.c which only sets ERR_SET).

Second thing is taking care of that for all three places I could find,
- garmin.c binary mode
- nmea_parse.c parsing PGRME
- libgpsd_core.c generating PGRME sentence
there's always CEP50_SIGMA used instead of just "2" in garmin.c which
introduced a rounding error.
