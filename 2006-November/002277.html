On Tue, 28 Nov 2006, Robert J. Berger wrote:
> Looks like the mailing list software drops the attachments or doesn't
> like my attachments.

List email from gpsd-users has among its headers

> X-Content-Filtered-By: Mailman/MimeDel 2.1.9

and I suspect that's why the attachments don't come through.  I help run
another mailing list that uses a similar filter program to remove
everything but the plain text part of mails sent to the list.  It tends
to keep a lot of the brain damage of certain well-known MUAs off of the
list.

Ways around it include CC'ing indviduals directly (if they have
indicated this is OK) and, for shorter text items, just including them
in the body of your mail with clear "---cut here---" markers.  You could
also put it on a Web page and provide a link, or maybe post it to one of
the sources groups on Usenet.
