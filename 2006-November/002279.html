yet another eye-candy generator. given 24 hrs of "Y" messages, this
tool produces a skyplot of accumulated signal strength. By way of
example, gpsd.mainframe.cx is on top of an office building, with
hardly any multipath to worry about. gpsd2.mainframe.cx is in an
office window, with buildings all around, so i have multipath and
obstructions. geographically, they're not that far apart.

Colour scheme: bright green for strong, dark green for good, yellow
for fair, red for weak. nothing if not tracking at all.

The tool is written in php and makes no attempt to be clever or
efficient; i just recycled a bunch of gpsd.php.

http://www.ualberta.ca/~ckuethe/gps/skyviews/
