> Sorry for my english skills, what does "noodling" mean?

A "noodle" is generally considered slang for "brain". For example,
when someone is being clever, one might say "now that's using your
noodle." To give something a noodle, or to noodle something is to
ponder, to consider or to think on the matter.

In other words, this individual is considering our request and
attempting to justify the business case.
