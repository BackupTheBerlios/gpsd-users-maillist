Halls of shame are not supposed to be nice warm fuzzy places. It's
there to embarrass the named entities and to cost them money. Welcome
to business.

I've been after garmin for years to document their protocol properly.
So has everybody who's ever bothered to read the garmin driver in
ntpd. Garmin does not deserve humor and irony. They listen to money,
and unless you're gonna blow a couple million dollars on buying
receivers, you're not getting docs from them. Or if you do, it won't
be under a reasonable license.

Globalsat may deserve to be on the list for selling a receiver that
they claim is WAAS-capable, and then wanting to charge me $10 to
reflash it with the right firmware.

We already have our own hall of shame - the gpsd-dev list and the
gpsd-commit-watch list. Try get a closed source vendor to admit how
badly they screwed the pooch sometime...
