On 11/18/06, Hossein Ghaffari Nik <hgn842001 at gmail.com> wrote:
> Am I going to be able to do this with GPSd?
> If yes, please guide me in the right direction and give me some insights in
> this process!

There is a utility in the gpsd distribution which can be used to do
this easily: gpspipe. You can log NMEA, gpsd or raw binary messages.

Before going too far, be sure your compiler isn't broken - we've had
some problems with buggy compilers for ARM systems (like gumstix). See
http://gpsd.berlios.de/faq.html

Unless floattest produces the following statement - "floating point
math appears to work" - you will probably get erroneous output from
gpsd.
