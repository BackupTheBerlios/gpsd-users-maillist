It's GPL so there's no way we're importing it. That being said, I have
no problem collaborating with them and sending patches and
recommendations. I've sent them some patches to make it compile on
OpenBSD (probably necessary for other BSDs as well).

They do things like testing
        #ifdef linux
rather than testing
        #ifdef NCURSES_ATTR_T
to see if ncurses_attr_t is defined. Basically, you can tell this was
written with one or two flavors of linux, and maybe an attempt at
windows in mind.

I now have it compiling and apparently running, though I haven't tried
anything useful with it. I suspect that will change once gpsd gets
RINEX output capability. Once we have RINEX, then we can talk to a
whole bunch of processing packages.
