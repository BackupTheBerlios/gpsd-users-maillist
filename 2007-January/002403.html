I believe it is not a good idea to use any available sql packages to store
gps data on the device itself because of "Wear Levelling" issues.

I also agree with what "Matt Roberds" says except porting issues, because it
depends on the person :). In example, I love to do that stuff and we are
using gpsd with our custom pxa270 board but it is up to you what hardware to
use, it is not relevant.

If you want to do some DIY thing, you may also use x86 pc104 boards
(http://www.icop.com.tw/). They can fit in a car.
