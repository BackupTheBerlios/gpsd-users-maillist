On Wed, 10 Jan 2007, Chris Kuethe wrote:

> Gary, your thoughts on using libusb instead of depending on the kernel m
> odule?

In theory it is a nice idea.  Libusb has done wonders for sane, pilot-xfer,
gphoto2 and a bunch of other projects.  Moving drivers out of the kernel
can only make the kernel more robust.

Balancing that out are the practicalities.  I have no idea how much
effort a project like moving garmin usb to libusb might be.  Whatever
effor that may be there are other things that don't work at all that
are probably higher priorities.

Does anyone know what OS that libusb supports?

If someone thought it a fun project to do then they should probably
dig right in.
