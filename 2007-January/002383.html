On Tue, 9 Jan 2007, Ulrich Voigt wrote:

> gpsd: speed 9600, 8N1
> gpsd: Can't open /proc/bus/usb/devices

That is not an error, unless you have a Garmin USB device.

> I think the problem is the "Can't open /proc/bus/usb/devices" line. As I
> understand the /proc/bus/usb directory is no longer supported by current
> libUSB versions.

gpsd does NOT use libusb.

> Does anyone know how to fix this? Is this a gpsd problem or a garmin kernel
> module problem?

If /proc/bus/usb/devices does not exist then there is something wrong with
your linux kernel configuration.  It is the job of the usb kernel modules
to maintain and populate /proc/bus/usb.
