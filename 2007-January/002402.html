Only the SL6000 has built-in wifi. All of the others can do it via a
CF card (and it works just fine). There might even be SDIO support at
some point (but not now, so don't try).

I would suggest the C3x00 if you want lots of storage space (C3200 if
you have enough money) or the C1000 if you can either run on the
internal flash or on a (gig or 2) SD card.
