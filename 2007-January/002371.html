On Wed, 3 Jan 2007, Ulrich Voigt wrote:

> I am having a similar problem with gpsd 2.34 and ntp 4.2.0a at 1.1196-r.
> I am running SuSE Linux 10.1 and my GPS receiver has a SiRF III chip.

You must run gpsd with the -n option for NTP to work.  Otherwise
gpsd connects to the gps device only when a client is connected.

Try starting gpsd this way:
	gpsd -nN -D 5 /dev/ttyS0

That will force gpsd to rin in the forground and log the NTP
action.
