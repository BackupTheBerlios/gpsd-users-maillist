> I downgraded to 2.33 and still the same thing. Did 2.34 change the
> protocol. gps number two shows NMEA output.
>
> What has happened and how can I regain use of gps number one?

Yes, gpsd switched to the binary protocol. Assuming gpsd can parse the
output from the tty, you can use the B and N commands to force the
speed and protocol back.

Failing that, take the batteries out of the misbehaving receiver for a
while; that should reset everything.
