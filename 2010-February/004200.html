Prior to the "Native POSIX Thread Library", when pthreads were used, there 
was an additional manager thread that also got created. If your kernel or 
thread library is old, that might be what is happening. Here is a link to an 
outdated, but informative document. 
http://people.redhat.com/drepper/nptl-design.pdf
