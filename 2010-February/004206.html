Hi Gary,

with a real serial port I'm routinely having ~<1 us precision.  Verified 
with several GPS sources on the same machine.

with a serial to USB converter to a device delivering PPS I have ~1 ms. 
The jitter reported by chronyd is about 125 us (ntpd would be ~the same) 
(average over filtered data for about 2 min), but with this one has to be 
careful.  It is just the USB 1 kHz clock slowly drifting vs the real clock 
that gives this apparent low value, there are 1 ms jumps once in a while 
in the offset.  It also has an average 1 ms offset, as expected from the 1 
kHz poll/irq latency.

Håkan
