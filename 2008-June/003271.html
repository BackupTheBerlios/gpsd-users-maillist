> First of all, thanks to all the people from #gpsd that help me running gpsd
> on my linux box. After a long series of testing I have succesfully connected
> my GARMIN GPSMAP 178C Sounder [1] to my linux box running gpsd and it show
> data with no problem: I get all the NMEA strings related to GPS.
> The problem is that I don't see any strings coming from the SONAR ( i.e.
> SBDDBT, SDDPT, SDMTW and SDVHW), also if the reciver show the right value of
> depth.

gpsd doesn't interpret those.  You'd have to write a device driver specifically
for that device to interpret them and pack them into the O report; then you'd
have to teach your client application to parse the O report in the right way.
