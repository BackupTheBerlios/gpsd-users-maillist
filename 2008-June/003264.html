On Sun, Jun 15, 2008 at 12:37 PM, Eric A <erpo41 at gmail.com> wrote:
> but I don't see anything on the hardware compatibility page relating
> to bluetooth GPS receivers. From what I can tell, it registers itself
> as a serial port on the connected laptop, so gpsd ought to be able to
> talk to it. Any thoughts?

gpsd will talk to bluetooth receivers. it may even talk too much.
sometimes the probe sequence causes the receiver to lock up. also,
baud rate switches can confuse the serial layer, leaving the device
incommunicado. to ameliorate these two conditions, gpsd has the "-b"
switch to prevent gpsd from ever saying anything that might cause a
receiver to freak out.
