Gary E. Miller wrote:
> I think all GPS will find there location in 15 mins
> tops, and in most cases a lot faster than that.


IIRC- a new almanac takes ~15 minutes to download, but you have to be
there to see it start. So in the worst case it could take 29.99999
minutes from power-on to have a fresh almanac saved, best case 15.00001.

But you don't need the almanac to get a fix, it just helps the GPS to
know where to look for the satellites, and if it knows exactly where to
look before hand, it can lock on to enough in minimal time (less than 30
sec).

This has implications if your app powers up the GPS only long enough to
get a fix (say 1 min) every 30 minutes or so. It's almanac never gets
updated. This is especially bad (long time to each fix; power wasting)
if you put fresh batteries in the unit before deployment without giving
it .5 hour powered up in the sun to get its first almanac. (oops)
