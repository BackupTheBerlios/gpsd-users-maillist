On 5/21/07, Gary E. Miller <gem at rellim.com> wrote:
> Piece of cake.  Just have your software GPS output NMEA to a named pipe.
> Then have gpsd read from that named pipe.  Something like this should work:
>
> mknod /tmp/gps p
> softgps > /tmp/gps &
> gpsd -nN /tmp/gps

Or, if you have /dev/stdin, you can do something like this:

softgps | ./gpsd -n -N -D3 /dev/stdin

(i used -D3 just so I could see that gpsd was in fact doing something)
