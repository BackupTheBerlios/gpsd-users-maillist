Yo Ingmar!

On Mon, 21 May 2007, Ingmar Frank wrote:

> would it be possible to use gpsd in conjunction with a software GPS?

Piece of cake.  Just have your software GPS output NMEA to a named pipe.
Then have gpsd read from that named pipe.  Something like this should work:

mknod /tmp/gps p
softgps > /tmp/gps &
gpsd -nN /tmp/gps
