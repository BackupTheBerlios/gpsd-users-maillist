Hi,

I also own a BU-353 and use it on open SuSE 10.2

It needs the following modules to be loaded

usbcore
usbserial
pl2303

The stuff that takes care of the loading is udev e.a.

try this:

modprobe -c > moduledata

it will give you a editable/searchable list (moduledata) with all the hardware to drivers links
known to the system
if it does not contain the 0x067b 0x2303 link to pl2303 you will never get a /dev/ttyUSBx

If the pl2303 driver is not on your system you may be in for a kernel update or compile.
Guess that is not a subject for this list.
