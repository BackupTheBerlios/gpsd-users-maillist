>The serial port argument you should use with GPSD is /dev/ttyS0 .
>
>To check what's coming out of the card, install "cutecom" as it's a very
>simple GUI based serial port terminal program (unless you are more comfy
>with minicom or it's ilk) and connect to /dev/ttyS0.  If you aren't
>getting data (using 8N1 serial format) change the baud rate from the
>default 4800 and turn off and flow control.  You should see the various
>NMEA reports coming out.
