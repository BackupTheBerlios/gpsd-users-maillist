> Any idea what to try on "the kernel/hotplug" problem  ???

It might just be the VID:PID of your device isn't recognised properly by
hotplug.

Your prolific USB device is: 067b:2303
Mine is: 4348:5523

Grep /etc for both.

I am just stabbing in the dark here but you just need to get hotplug to do
the same thing when both these VID:PIDs are presented.  lsusb just looks
up the usb.ids file to show the string against which the VID:PID is
attached to - it doesn't mean hotplug had gone and done something useful.
