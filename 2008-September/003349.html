Open a shell, type "tail -f /var/log/messages" (ctrl-c when you are done)
and watch what gets printed in the log when you plug the card in.  USB
serial devices usually say that they are being assigned to /dev/ttyUSBx
where x is a number.  You might get some clues then as to what serial
driver might be loaded and what system device it's associated with.
