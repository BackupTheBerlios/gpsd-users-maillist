On Tue, 5 Sep 2006 22:25:21 -0400, Eric S. Raymond wrote
> A gpsd release is imminent, unless I hear within the next day or so
> about some issue serious enough to be a release blocker.
> 
> And here's a service question: does anybody who's listening know how
> to build 32-bit RPMS on a 64-bit machine?

You have two main options, either a 32-bits chrooted build env, or a
cross-compiling toolchain, just like when you're building for another
architecture.

You can also have all your build tools and libs duplicated (64 bits + 32 bits
versions) but that is even bigger work and has little difference from a full
cross-compiling toolchain.
