Yo George!

On Fri, 29 Sep 2006, xterm wrote:

> I 've managed to make gpsd work with Novatel oem4 gps receivers. I 've just
> changed the serial pin from dcd to cts for pps.I am writing commands to gpsd
> telnet port and i am getting the right time from the nmea messages.

For PPS to work on CTS you have to be using the latest SVN. 2.33 is not
good enough.  You also need to set '--enable-pps-on-cts' in the config .

It goes without saying that you are runnig both ntpd and gpsd as root.

I suspect the problem is in your ntp or ntp.conf.  Even if gpsd has never
run you should see SHM(0) and SHM(1) in the "ntpdc -p".  Double check
your config and be sure you have all the clock drivers compiled in to
ntpd.
