On Thu, 28 Sep 2006, Tais M. Hansen wrote:

> xgps says it has a fix every 5 seconds but quickly changes to "no fix" just
> after. Kismet shows similar behavior. GPSDrive, however, doesn't seem to
> behave like this. And the GPS device itself works fine with TomTom on my
> Ipaq.

This is a known issue.  Some gpsd clients want every drib of information
as soon as gpsd gets it, other clients just want the lost complete fix even
if that adds a buffer delay.

What you are seeing is the data exacly as the GPS sends it to gpsd.  What
you are looking for is just the last complete fix.  There is a resolution
to this issue in process.  Look for it at the next gpsd release.
