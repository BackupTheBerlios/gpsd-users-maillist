Is this behavior another manifestation of the data holdover issue that
you are trying to fix with the "j" command. The gps sends altitude in
the NMEA data but the altitude gets reset to "unknown" when a sentence
without altitude is read. As Gary reports elsewhere, about 90% of the
time (because of the sentence timing), altitude is unknown and reported
as "?". This behavior seems to be new since 2.32. I think it appeared
in, at least, the release version of 2.33. I am not sure if I can go
back and check 2.32, but I will try.
