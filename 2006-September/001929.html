On Wed, 6 Sep 2006, Massimo Burcheri wrote:

> > gpsd: <= GPS: $PMGNST,05.40,2,T,927,08.0,+04890,00*42
> > gpsd: unknown sentence: "$PMGNST,05.40,2,T,927,08.0,+04890,00*42\x0d
> > \x0a"

You can find info on that here:
	http://www.gpsinformation.org/dale/nmea.htm

- ---
  $PMGNST,02.12,3,T,534,05.0,+03327,00*40

where:
      ST      status information
      02.12   Version number?
      3       2D or 3D
      T       True if we have a fix False otherwise
      534     numbers change - unknown
      05.0    time left on the gps battery in hours
      +03327  numbers change (freq. compensation?)
      00      PRN number receiving current focus
      *40    checksum
- ---

gpsd does not need use that for anything.  Unless you need the battery
charge info I see nothing in there of use to gpsd.
