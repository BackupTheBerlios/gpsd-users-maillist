Looking at the GPS-18/LVC spec.

Section 1.5.3: The unit accepts TIA-232-F (RS-232) level inputs and
transmits voltage levels that swing from ground to the positive supply
coltage, TIA-232-F (RS-232) polarity.

Section 1.6.2.1: Input Voltage
  GPS-18/LVC: 4.0 - 5.5V

Section 1.6.2.2 Input Current
  GPS-18/LVC: 60mA @ 5.0V

Section 1.6.2.3 CMOS Serial Output Levels
  GPS-18/LVC: 0V to Vin, between 4 and 5.5V (Asynchronous Serial, TIA-232-F
              (RS-232) Compatible Polarity)

You should check your spec, but I would think you could drive 3.3V logic
directly with this.
