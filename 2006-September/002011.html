Yo Dave!

If you are serious about the GPS-18/LVC I have been checking on some code
to SVN to use the GPS in Garmin Serial Binary mode.  It is still flakey
so you may want to hold off some more.  There are few advantages to the
binary mode.  One advantage is that the messages are fixed length.  That
means the offset from "real" time to when I decode the PVT message is
pretty stable.  Stable to without maybe +/- 10mS so even without PPS
I can get a fair time stamp from it now.

The humidity at my office is often 10% or lower, but not a lot of static
problems here.  I have been at places with static problems and then you
have to be real careful.  My experience with static is that most of the
experts are just wrong.  Things like pink poly, heel straps, and wrist
straps are just useless.  The US Mil-Std is right, the only worksatation
anti-stat measure with a good change of working is an ion-generator
pointed directly at the workspace.
