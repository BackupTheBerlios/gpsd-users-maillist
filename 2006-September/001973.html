> I can't find anywhere to set the device to send NMEA.

That is correct.  Garmins with USB, of all flavors, never speak NMEA.
There is no way around it.

> Here is the output of the command gpsd -n -N -D3 /dev/ttyUSB0

And that looks fairly good.  Packets are moving and being decoded properly.

> It keeps going, but I cut it off after a few sentences

> gpsd: launching (Version 2.30)

2.33 is current, although I know of no changes that should affect this.

> gpsd: Garmin Product ID: 484, SoftVer: 2.40
> gpsd: Garmin Product Desc: Forerunner305 Software Version 2.40

I just grabbed the newest Garmin doc today, and that prduct ID is not
listed in their technical manual.  The fact that the product description
was decoded is a good sign.  That the product ID is not documented is not
so good.
