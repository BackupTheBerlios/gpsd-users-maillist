I've just tried updating to a significantly newer version of gpsd,

source .tar.gz - on Mac OS X, so no RPMing) - exactly as I had done 
gpsd-2.6 (which worked perfectly)... but now it just doesn't want to

the first command sent to it (no response... although it doesn't drop 
the connection).  Having been unable to reproduce the bug in 2.7 when
