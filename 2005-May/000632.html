Remco and Rob Janssen are still trying to figure out why gpsd is
reporting times to Remco (but not to Rob or me) that are ten seconds
off.  Otherwise Remco's flurry of bug reports has been dealt with,
fixes are in SVN head.

We have a bug report of gpsd losing its devicename pointer after five
hours of continuous operation.  I have not been able to reproduce
this.  I believe the person who reported it is now working on pinning
down the version that introduced it.

Alfredo <alfredio at fastwebnet.it> has sent a patch that adds a facility
for true threaded callbacks.  I haven't merged this yet because he changes 
the user-visible API.

Otherwise the bug and feature queue is clear.  Importantly, I have
confirmation from one of the people who reported it that the crazy-speed
bug is dead.

Seems like a good timme for a checkpoint release, unless somebody
has a bug in the showstopper/must-fix category.
