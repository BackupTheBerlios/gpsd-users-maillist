it might be gpsdrive's version of gpsd!
make sure you don't have both versions in your path (`echo $PATH`), if
yes i recommand renaming one of them to gpsd.gpsdrive or gpsd.svn
`which gpsd` will tell you which binary is used,
check /usr/sbin, /usr/local/sbin, /usr/bin and /usr/local/sbin for gpsd
binaries.
