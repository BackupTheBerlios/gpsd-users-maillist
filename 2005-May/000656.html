What kind of simulation are you after? You could get someone with a
laptop to go drive around for a while and dump the NMEA into a file.
You could write a script to synthesize NMEA output and parse that. You
could keep a database of SiRF messages that could be replayed at gpsd.

Off the top, I'd say you could fake it with a script that outputs a
static skyview every 5 seconds, and maps a simple loopy path to real
coordinates.
