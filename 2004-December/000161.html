IIRC, GPS altitude is hight above/below the WGS84 geoid model, not
necessarily sea level. Also note that vertical GPS accuracy is about an
order of magnitude worse than in the horizontal.

so +/- 20m in the vertical isn't anything to worry about (unless you are
in an airplane trying to land in no-viz).

The more satellites it sees, the less the error will be (hopefully).
