  11.  Notes on the device
This waterproof GPS receiver connects to a USB port on a laptop or
computer. 

photo http://linuxathome.ath.cx/~steve/techway-tp51.jpg

Features

-Support high sensitivity for very weak signal acquisition and tracking.
-Integrated powerful CPU and base band hardware in an System On Chip
(SOC) design reducing the space and power consumption in the state of
the art. 
-Advanced IC technology that makes the total receiver cost saving. 
-Allows the receiver to track the satellites using up to 12-channel
correlators. 
-Fast TTFF for cold start, warm start and hot start. 
-High performance firmware drives an excellent positioning speed. 
-Dual serial communication channels and user selectable baud rates 
-Designed with a maximum interface capability and flexibility. 
-Optional downloadable firmware code support for upgrading into advanced
programs. 
-Supports a backup power to sustain the internal clock. 
-USB cable connector. 

Compatibility 

Fits into any computer/laptop with a PS/2 connection, or with a USB port
using a PS/2 to USB converter

Available Options 

PS/2 to USB cable converter


Specifications 

-Channels: 12 channel all-in-view tracking
-Position: 5 w/o S/A
-Time: 1 micro-second synchronized to GPS time

Mechanical: 
-Dimension: 33(W) x 33(L) x 10(H) mm
-Operating Temperature: -20oC~ 70oC
-Humidity: 5 to 95% non-condensing
-Velocity Limit: 500 meters/sec. 
-Altitude Limit: 18,000 meters (60,000 feet) max.

Power Consumption: 
-Power Supply: 5.0V DC and up to 9.0V DC
-Power Consumption: 120mW

Satellite Acquisition: 
-Acquisition: 0.1 sec. average 
-Hot start: Hot start: 2 sec (Min.) 
-Warm start: Warm start: 35 sec (Average)
-Cold start:: 50 sec (Average)
-NMEA 0183 and WGS84 Datum
-GPS Protocol: GGA, GSV, GSA, RMC,(optional VTG,GLL)
