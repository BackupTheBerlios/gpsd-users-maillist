* The berlios.de listserv silently strips off attachments. And has done
so for many months.

* Posting patches to the list as in-line text sucks, as then it will
get line-wrapped by the mailer/reader and fail to apply cleanly.

* Even if the attachment gets to the individual recipient by direct cc,
it is lost to review by others or saved in the archives.

* This is a real hindrance to development, with dubious anti-spam
benefit.
