> On Sun, 13 Jan 2008, Gary E. Miller wrote:
>> On Sun, 13 Jan 2008, John Arthur wrote:
>>
>>> Please find attached a patch to make gpsd/pps work with a Garmin 18-5Hz.
>> No patch attached.
> The berlios.de copy did not have an attachment, but the direct copy did.

This seems to trip people up about once every month or two on gpsd-users.
I think I agree with disallowing attachments (it stops bad things like
HTML mail), but perhaps a short note could be added to the boilerplate
trailer about it?  Something like:

---
Gpsd-users mailing list        NOTE: No attachments (patches, etc) allowed!
Gpsd-users at lists.berlios.de    Mail patches directly to $SOMEWHERE.
https://lists.berlios.de/mailman/listinfo/gpsd-users
---

$SOMEWHERE might be "a gpsd maintainer", or an email address set up for
the purpose, or something.
