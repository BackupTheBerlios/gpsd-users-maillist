On Tue, 23 Mar 2010, Ryan Turcotte 10E wrote:

> I've discovered USB GPS dongles are pretty terrible for usage with ntpd

Yep.  Unless all you need is to the nearest second.

>  It's not the delay, it's too much random jitter. The offset spans the

> Does anyone have any experience with PCMCIA cards? What would their
> ntpd delays be?

They will be about the same as the USB dongle.  The jitter/delay is in
the serial stream itself and from the sender.

> http://www.mightygps.com/pccard.htm says "1 microsecond synchronized to

IFF you can get PPS from your GPS then that should be very doable.
