Hi Yan,
> The further west I go, the greater the deviation to the
> south.  50 miles west of here, gpsd reports a position
> some 17 miles south of the actual position.  Here it's
> only about a mile.
> 
> Does anyone have any idea what could be causing this? 
> It's really, really bizzare.


check that some part of your processing software is not mis-
reading degrees+minutes as simply decimal degrees.

e.g. 44 deg 59 minutes is 44 + 59/60, and not 44.590.
error ranges from 0 at 44.0 latitude, to 40 nautical miles out
at 44d59'59.9999".

also as a rule never trust computer maps to be registered
correctly. double check against an official paper map if you
can. maybe your gps is fine and the software is buggy.
