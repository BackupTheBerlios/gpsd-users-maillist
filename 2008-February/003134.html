On Feb 3, 2008 4:17 PM, Mark Easton <mark63 at azurebell.co.nz> wrote:
> I switch to using gpsd in watcher mode (I assume that means the same thing
> as using the callback), and it is now working great. You were right Chris.

Not quite - watcher mode just means gpsd pushes fixes. On the client
side you can use a libgps callback to have some function executed
every time gpsd sends you a fix, or you can select()/read() yourself
if that would suit your application better. But we do try make watcher
mode and callbacks play nice together to save you from having to worry
about that logic yourself.
