I plan on shipping gpsd-2.37 in one week. Test and speak now or
forever hold your peace. I've had pretty good luck with telling random
people in irc "try svn, come back if it doesn't work" - it invariably
works. Two things to note:
 * I disabled the Garmin Simple Text Protocol because it causes SiRF
packets to mis-detect as NMEA. I'll be happy to work with GSTP users
after release to get this enabled, but working SiRF takes precedence
over GSTP.
 * I added back C++ binding support because a) it works for me, b) the
complaint that caused this to be disabled was eventually traced back
to a broken toolchain, and c) there is apparently some mapping
application in KDE4 that wants the C++ binding to libgps.

release candidate snapshot here -
