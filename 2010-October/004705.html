On Fri, 27 Aug 2010, Tomalak Geret'kal wrote:

> I'm running gpsd and ntpd on an embedded platform. The machine may be deployed
> in the field where NTP servers are inaccessible, and the machine may boot with
> a wildly inaccurate hardware clock.

Than you do NOT want to use ntpd.  ntpd is designed for continuous
connection to many time servers.

You want to use chrony.  chrony is designed for intermittent net access.
