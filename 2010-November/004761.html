On 19/11/2010 18:14, Gary E. Miller wrote:
>> If no ephemeris has been received within the last 30 minutes from any SV, the
>> almanac is used to pick a satellite for first position fix.
>
> Then that would account for a 30 minute cold-start delay?
>
> And how long to download the almanac?  On a cold start both the
> ephemeris and almanac are out of date.  I tend to lump them together,
> but you are correct that they are different items.

Gary,

The almanac is in subframes 4-5 of the same signal 
(ephemeris is found in subframes 2-3), so the same scenario 
applies.

The 30 minute value is the minimum duration that the device 
was offline, not a figure to be counted after the device is 
restarted.
