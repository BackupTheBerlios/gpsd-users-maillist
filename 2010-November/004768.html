Yo Tomalak!

On Fri, 19 Nov 2010, Tomalak Geret'kal wrote:

> > The frame formats are fixed, but the other items, like repeat rates and
> > actual number of satellites in the constellation (thus the size of the
> > ephemeris) are not.
>
> Also, it was my understanding that SVs only transmitted their own ephemeris.

Yes, each SV only transmits its own FULL ephemeris (section 30.3.3.1.1
).  In addition each sat transmits the reduced precision ephemeris,
called the Reduced Almanac (message 31 or 12) and the Midi Almanac
(message 37), for the full constellation (section 30.3.3.4 ). So each SV
is transmitting the reduced precision ephemeris for all SVs.

Sadly the term 'ephermeris' in the spec is not used consistently in the
spec.  Their definition is all data for all sats, but then they use the
term as shorthand for the individual full ephemeris data for individual sats
and also applied to the reduced precision epehermeris data from the
almanacs when describing the calculations.
