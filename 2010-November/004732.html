On Sun, 7 Nov 2010, Pat Suwalski wrote:

> Does anyone know if a Garmin GPS 18x USB works with gpsd?

I have a Garmin GPS-18/USB.  Not the 18x, but it works great.  I see no
change in the 18x that should break anything and have heard many success
reports.

> I have the same problem. The module garmin_gps creates ttyUSB0, but nothing
> shows up in minicom; gpsd doesn't seem to see it either, there isn't any
> output from "gpscat /dev/ttyUSB0".

The Garmin 18 does NOT output ASCII, it is binary only so, so minicom will
not work.

Due to the bizarre Garmin protocol I would not expect gpscat too work either.

> I am using Ubuntu 10.10, Debian Squeeze, and Angstrom.
