To be precise, the GPS Signal is transmitted over a period 
of 12.5 minutes. You should only ever have to wait up to 20 
minutes or so, in the worst case, to receive ephemeris for 
three SVs. If the Garmin GPS 18 takes half an hour, I can't 
understand why.

If no ephemeris has been received within the last 30 minutes 
from any SV, the almanac is used to pick a satellite for 
first position fix.
