There are three separate issues to deal with:

* gpsd goes away, the problem at hand.

* gpsd continues to run, but loses contact with the GPS receiver (the
  user unplugs it, e.g.). I have also solved that one.

* The hardware and software continue to run correctly but the receiver
  loses all satellites and can't produce a fix (e.g. you are in a
  tunnel. [OK, not likely in your application]). That's up to you but
  you should have a graceful way to handle it.
