I further investigated this and I think it has to do with ownership and
creation of gpsd.sock
$ ls -l gpsd.sock
srwxr-xr-x 1 root root 0 2005-07-11 15:02 /var/run/gpsd.sock

>From gpsd.hotplug I understand it should be srw-rw---
and, because /dev/ttyUSB0 is owned by root.dialout I suppose it should
also be owned by root.dialout

So in gpsd.hotplug the following did not work for some reason:
os.chmod(argument, stat.S_IMODE(os.stat(argument)[stat.ST_MODE]) | 0660)
But why?

BTW I got a user gpsd who is in the group dialout.
