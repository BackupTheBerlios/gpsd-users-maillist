I just installed gpsd 2.28 on my (mostly) Debian Sarge laptop. It seemed
to work Ok, but when I shut-down the laptop and later wanted to restart
it (with the gps attached through a PL-2303 serial-USB converter) the
start-up hangs with the last messages:

and then nothing happened anymore

After I did [Ctrl]c I got this:

gpsd" Then:
# stty -F /dev/ttyUSB0 ispeed 4800 && cat < </dev/ttyUSB0

# stty -F /dev/ttyUSB0 ispeed 4800 && cat < </dev/ttyUSB0
