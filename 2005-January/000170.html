Huzzah!!

For the record, the previous problems still remain somewhat a mystery. 
Something in my off-the-shelf Slackware kernel didn't work with the 
ThinkPad's PnP serial port. A trimmed-down custom kernel (2.6.9) did 
the job; setserial(8) recognized the UART, and gpsd ... worked! :) So 
did gpsdrive using the serial port directly. No problems at all since 
getting a good custom kernel built.

1. Name: Garmin Geko 201
    http://www.garmin.com/products/geko201/spec.html
    http://www.garmin.com/manuals/Geko201_OwnersManual.pdf
(specifications are Appendix A, page 34, the 42nd page of the PDF)
    http://www.garmin.com/support/commProtocol.html
