On Wed, 19 Jan 2005, Brandon Coleman wrote:

> I am having several problems with my Garmin USB GPS 18 receiver.

Have you patched your kernel with the Garmin driver?  Get it here:
	http://sourceforge.net/projects/garmin-gps/

Not perfect, but it gets the job done for me.

The driver will tell you which /dev/ttyUSB to use once it sees the GPS 18.

The USB serial pretends to be a UART to the /dev.  It ignores the
speed setting since it is really running at 10mbps.
