Here's a patch that restores the lost functionality in the last SVN
Basically, it tells the zodiac driver to look for NMEA triggers when
receiving a non-zodiac formatted packet. So when the zodiac driver
receives EARTHA, it falls back to the earthmate driver (which in turns
calls the zodiac driver, but that's another story).

It seems to do the trick for me, I hope it doesn't break anything :-)
