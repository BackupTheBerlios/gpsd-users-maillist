Please send the list a raw ASCII capture of the NMEA from that GPS.  Then
someone can use that to test a fix.

> I also noticed that cgps was toggling between 3D Fix and No Fix, which
> is probably related to this.

This is a side effect of the NMEA protocol.  gpsd compbines all the data
from the current time time stamp to its position reports.  Early in the
cycle the NMEA has not been sent yet to denote a 3D fix.  Check out the
list archives for spirited discussions on the issue.
