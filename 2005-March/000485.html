Russ is also correct in that pointing at other people who got this wrong
won't win the argument.  The truth is that your cast-and-pack method 
will fail miserably unless 

(a) The machine is byte- rather than word- addressable.

(b) Structures have no alignment requirements 

That's *why* gcc -W issues a warning.  Fail to heed it at your peril.
