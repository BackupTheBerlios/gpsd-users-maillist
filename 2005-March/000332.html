This is a big deal in naviagation software on ships. Just about all
software assumes GPS fix is for the fixed antenna, and wants to know
where on the ship the antenna is. The ship's outline is then drawn based
length, bredth, and distance from the bow of the GPS antenna. This is a
major (if not THE major) market for embedded gps (+often linux) systems.
Locating the garbage truck fleet is still early days; ships have been
doing GPS for 10+ years and it is much more mature. Don't ignore them.
