Hello Paolo,

If you want to use it professionally and need the sturdy built of the Garmin,
stay with it. You can connect a computer via the RS-232 (at least the spec.
says so), so you do not lose anything compared to the Leadtek.

I have not tested the Leadtek, but some other similar ones (Holux, Globalsat,
etc.). They are not bad and are nice if you want to design a complete system,
but they are just consumer items.
