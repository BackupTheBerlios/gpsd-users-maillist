> > > As an aside, how come all these receivers have such a low max
> > > speed?  

Because there's little point in having higher speeds than about 9600
at normal data volumes - an NMEA device simply doesn't generate enough
bytes per cycle to fill the pipe.  See my white paper on profiling
performance for graphs and measurements showing this.

Enabling subframe data would change this, but I'd be surprised if it
pushed the threshould speed above 19200.
