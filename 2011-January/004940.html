Oh yes you can calculate whatever date you want, day of week, day of
month, phase of moon, Gregorian or Julian whatever.  Trick is to treat
the start as the 'epoch' and do some modulus arithmetic  e.g divide by
7 gives you the day of the week, by 28 the phase of the moon etc.
When I did the date routines for the GE 210 computers back in the
1960's  I used an algorithm gleaned from the ACM library of
algorithms.  Google is your friend as it has been a while since the GE
computers have been current.
