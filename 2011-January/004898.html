OOC, did you really get to the official NMEA Standard this way? I do not
mean to the place where you can buy the standard or become a member of
the NMEA group. The standard, as far as I know, is not publicly
available.

If you mean "http://www.tronico.fi/OH6NT/docs/NMEA0183.pdf", well that
is not the standard. It is some guy's personal description of what he
thinks is the standard. It is always interesting to see what others have
found, but at the end of the day, all you have is an opinion. The
standard itself is not listed as a document in the References.
