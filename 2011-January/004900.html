I guess I mean that 'the consensus on the internet' is always an
interesting concept. I have no doubt that you will find references to 80
characters. But in my experience, it is more often the case that the
length of the line is not described. What is described generically for a
line is that it starts with a '$' and ends with a CR/LF. And that all
commas must be present as they are delimiters.

What gpsd does in this respect is of course up to the good folk that
maintain it. I can only report what I see in some newer high-end devices
from major suppliers like Trimble, Applanix and OxTS. I have no control
over what might eventually be done with this information.

I hope my information on these devices has been useful. Please feel free
to ask for any further details.
