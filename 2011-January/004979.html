> Hi everyone,
> 
> I try to setup a Trimble Thunderbolt GPS device. It uses the TSIP protocoll, but with NONE parity.
> 
> gpsd doesn't recognize this and tries to speak with it in 8O1 Mode, which won't work.
> 
> (See also: http://lists.berlios.de/pipermail/gpsd-users/2009-January/003562.html)
> 
> But how can I tell gpsd to use 8N1 Mode? Forcing the tty didn't work ("stty ignpar -F /dev/ttyS0"). 

It shouldn't be necessary to force this, as 8N1 is the first
combination tried in the normal autobaud loop.  To check this, try
running the daemon at -D 5 and watching the autobaud progress.
