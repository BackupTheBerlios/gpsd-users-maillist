On 15/01/2011 14:33, Ahmed Ramadan wrote:
> Yaaa, No more segmentation error, thanks charles :) ...
> but i have still a problem which is that, query status and fix status is
> always "0" , don't know why and the longitude and latitude and even time is
> "nan" ,,, despite i monitor the NEMA messages using "cat /dev/ttyS2" and i
> can see the longitude and latitude and everything is going well ... but my
> client gives "nan" instead :S do you know what is the reason ?

Your monitoring is the (or at least, one) reason.

The virtual devices on Linux such as /dev/ttyS2 are not 
multiplexers, so only one application may read from them at 
one time. When you do `cat /dev/ttyS2`, all the NMEA from 
your GPS is going to `cat`, and gpsd gets nothing.

Let gpsd be the only thing using `/dev/ttyS2`. If you want 
to observe the NMEA stream, use the `gpsmon` utility that 
gets the NMEA stream through gpsd.
