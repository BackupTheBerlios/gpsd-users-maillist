>
> Your monitoring is the (or at least, one) reason.
>
> The virtual devices on Linux such as /dev/ttyS2 are not multiplexers, so
> only one application may read from them at one time. When you do `cat
> /dev/ttyS2`, all the NMEA from your GPS is going to `cat`, and gpsd gets
> nothing.
>
> Let gpsd be the only thing using `/dev/ttyS2`. If you want to observe the
> NMEA stream, use the `gpsmon` utility that gets the NMEA stream through
> gpsd.
