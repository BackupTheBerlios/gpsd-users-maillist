I don't think any of us have seen the actual NMEA 0183 spec, but it is
widely believed the maximum length of a standard NMEA sentence is 80
characters. If someone can tell us whether or not that is true, we'd
appreciate it. And maybe tell us where else our NMEA parser is being
too strict or liberal.

We have not received competent legal advice indicating that we'd be
allowed to write a compliant open-source parser after having seen the
actual specification. We suspect that we'd get some kind of trouble,
so we're not going to go there.
