> A similar question arises when examining the xgps application. I
> gets a mix of "GND" and "MID2" messages back from gpsd. And, again,
> I can find no information that describes the NMEA sentence(s) that
> map to a MID2 response.

The tag for a sentence is generated by the driver for the device type
you are using, thus this report tells me you're using a SiRF chip.
The GND and MID tags aren't NMEA at all; they're associated with
SiRF binary sentences.
