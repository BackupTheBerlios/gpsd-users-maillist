Hi Stefan,

I suspect that you're not going to have much luck plugging the device 
into a multi-card reader.

CF storage cards can function in two modes - either in native PCMCIA 
Card modes, or as an IDE HDD. My experiences with multi-card readers is 
that they put the CF cards into IDE/HDD mode and read them that way.

My suspicions are that your card only functions in CF mode (as it's not 
a pure storage card, and it's probably not that easy to map a GPS as a 
HDD) and that you will need to plug it into a PCMCIA slot on a laptop to 
get it to function correctly.

Note also that there are differences between PCMCIA/Cardbus/PC Card 
style sockets, as well as PCMCIA Type I/II/III cards.

good luck.

ciao, Dave
