For anyone that was following this, the problem ended up being a problem
with the private driver union structure in gpsd.h. I didn't realize that
all of the driver private housekeeping data was inside of a union and so
since my driver was using information from both nmea and my own driver,
there was a place where the data was overlapping in a critical fashion.
