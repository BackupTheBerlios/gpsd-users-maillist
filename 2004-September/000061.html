>    I am trying to make GlobalSat BC-307 GPS card over iPAQs running Familiar OS (5.2). My iPAQ was able to recognize GPS card as serial driver but the problem is that I cannot make it to work. When my application try to connect to 2947 port and issue "p\n" command, gpsd gives back "GPSD,P=?". 

That is what it will do when your GPS has no fix.
 
> Does this line "pgpsd: GPS has no fix (status=0, mode=1): mean that gpsd is not able to connect to the GPS infrastructure.

No, it means your GPS has not got a satellite lock and can therefore not return
any valid information.
