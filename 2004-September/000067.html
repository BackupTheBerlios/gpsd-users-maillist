> This indicates that GPS has no fix since the second element after $GPGSA is 1.

>>$GPGSV,2,1,07,30,77,084,,06,64,221,40,05,42,101,,25,38,302,*7D

>>$GPGSV,2,2,07,17,35,126,,24,32,058,,14,16,238,*4A
> 
> ...
> More, your GPS receives a signal from only one satellite of 7 (satellite 
> number  06 - azimuth 221 deg, elevation 64, deg signal strength 40dB). This 
> happens if an antenna has poor view of sky.
