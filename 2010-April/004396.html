rabin shahav <shahav at gmail.com>:
> the problem in my system is that gpsd starts before the device become
> active.

That's fine.  As we explained before, here's how that should work:

1. You start gpsd

2. You plug in the device.

3. The hotplug system notices you have a USB-serial chip on our hotlist.

4. It sends a notification to gpsd's control port announcing the new device.

5. gpsd puts the device in its pool *but does not open it*

6. Later, an application opens a GPD session.  *then* the device is opened.

Now, this may fail if your USB-serial chip is not on our hotlist.  What
was your make and model again?  And what dous lusb show when you plug it in?
