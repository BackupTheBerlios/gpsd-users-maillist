Hi,

I've been giving some thought on dbus integration and I want to share a thought.

My phone is a Palm Pre and it uses dbus extensively.  When the phone
rings, an SMS comes in, talking to the accelerometer, turning the GPS
on/off, and getting GPS fixes all come through DBUS.

One thing is different, though, compared to what gpsd does now:
commands (or queries) and responses all take strings in and out.

Those strings are in the form of - JSON messages.  This means that
they remain self-describing, without having to depend on the crazy
dbus introspection mechanism.

Just a thought.
