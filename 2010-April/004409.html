Dunno if you are on Linux, xBSD, or what.  To generalize, gpsd does NOT
create the devices, the OS, or OS helper does that.  On linux it is
usually done by udev these days.

> I suppose gpsd always uses ttyUSB0 (or creates a ttyUSB1), but other apps
> seems to be missing the "link" sometimes...

gpsd has no default, that is assigned by whatever hotplu system your
OS may or may nbot use.

> I do not know where this gps0 comes from, is it libgps??

Libgps does not assign devices either.  it is just a library for
finding and using them.
