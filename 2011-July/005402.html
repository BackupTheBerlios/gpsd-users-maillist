The reaction times described there are for invisible stimuli that, when
made visible, must be reacted to. In my case, the stimuli are visible
before the reaction is required. The person is more predicting when a
visible stimuli moving at a constant rate will be at a certain location.
With training, the reaction time is much better than 190 msecs. Perhaps
I should be calling it 'prediction time' so as not to confuse the
paradigm.
