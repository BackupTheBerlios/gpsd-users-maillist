I just had a thought...maybe you can do it in software.  

Here's the deal.  If you can tell your application the filename of the
port device to use, write a little daemon that takes two arguments; a
pseudo-port name to sniff, and a real port to connect it to.  Open the
pseudo-port as a pty, pass though all data from the pseudo-port to the
real one and back -- then tell your app to talk to the pseudo-port.

If I needed this, I think I could write, test and document it in about
a day and a half in Python.
