very simple to make, $1.50 parts from Dick Smith/Radio Shack/wherever..

[do so at your own risk, this is off the top of my head, yada yada yada...]

2x DB9 female
1x DB9 male
some ribbon cable

pass through pins 2,3,5 from DB9-F to Male, wire second DB9-F with pins
2 and 5 connected to the same on the first one. Let first serial port
talk as normal, listen in on the second one. If you want to be fancy,
add a switch so pin 2 of the listening connector connects to pin 3 (Tx)
of the pass-thru DB9-F. Then you can listen in either direction, as
needed. Or add another DB9-F, so you have one for both Rx and Tx from
the computer- but I worry that you'll drain the signal voltage too much
if you add too many listening devices..? Don't try and be tricky and
wire Rx & Tx together to listen on both at the same time... (maybe with
signal diodes, but better not to try)

hardest part is finding a computer now-days with 2 serial ports.
(it's nice to use a common ground)
