I wrote: 
> RS-232 is only rated for 25 feet or so. Yes, lowering the baud rate
> can help. For longer distances you'll want to use RS-422. It's easy to
> find a little self powered black box 232<->422 converter to plug in at
> either end of the span. It wouldn't hurt to use well shielded thick
> cable & match your twisted pairs as well.

another tip: a 25 foot mains-power extension cord with the ends chopped
off makes for a nice long serial cable (Rx/Tx/Gnd only).
